Himachal Pradesh Statehood Day

Key Facts About Himachal Pradesh

The Prime Minister greeted the people of Himachal Pradesh on the occasion of Statehood Day, celebrated on 25th January.

History and Milestones

  • 1948: The Chief Commissioner’s province of Himachal Pradesh (HP) was formed on 15th April.
  • 1950: HP became a part C state with the implementation of the Constitution of India on 26th January.
  • 1956: HP became a Union Territory on 1st November after the State Reorganisation Commission’s recommendation.
  • 1966: Kangra and other hill areas of Punjab were merged with HP, but it remained a Union Territory.
  • 1971: The State of HP Act was passed on 18th December, and HP became the 18th state of India on 25th January.
