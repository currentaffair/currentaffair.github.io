Women’s Workforce Budget Needs

Context

Women’s groups call for a budget that boosts female workforce participation, emphasizing policies like employment-linked incentives, flexible work hours, and supportive infrastructure (e.g., work site crèches).

Praise for Gender Budget

  • Gender Budget Increase: ₹3 lakh crore for FY25.
  • Special Schemes: Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana and Namo Drone Didi focus on women’s financial empowerment.

Addressing Women’s Unique Needs

Organizations stress the importance of addressing women and girls' unique needs, particularly in skilling, livelihoods, and the care economy.

Support Infrastructure Proposals

  • Workplace Crèches: Proposals to develop crèches at workplaces.
  • Public Transport: Improved public transport systems for women.
  • Equal Pay Initiatives: Calls for initiatives to ensure equal pay for women.

Female Labour Force Participation

India’s female labour force participation rate stands at 32.7% in 2023, highlighting the need for more job opportunities for women.

Job Quality Focus

The 2025-26 budget is urged to focus on improving job quality and providing women with access to decent, dignified employment.

Optimism for Policy Changes

Experts, like Neera Nundy from Dasra India, express hope for favorable policies and enhanced tax incentives to support women-led initiatives.
