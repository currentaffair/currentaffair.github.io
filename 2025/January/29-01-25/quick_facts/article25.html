INCOIS Wins Disaster Management Award

Context

The Indian National Centre for Ocean Information Services (INCOIS) was selected for the Subhash Chandra Bose Aapda Prabandhan Puraskar-2025, recognizing its excellence in disaster management.

About the Subhash Chandra Bose Aapda Prabandhan Puraskar

  • Purpose: To recognize contributions in disaster management in India.
  • Announcement Date: Announced every 23rd January, on the birth anniversary of Subhash Chandra Bose.
  • Eligibility: Indian nationals and institutions working in disaster management fields.
  • Screening Process: Applications evaluated by a committee from NDMA.
  • Prize for Institutions: ₹51 lakh cash prize and a certificate of recognition.
  • Prize for Individuals: ₹5 lakh cash prize and a certificate of recognition.

About INCOIS

  • Parent Organization: Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES).
  • Location: Hyderabad, India.
  • Established: 1999.
  • Affiliation: Unit of Earth System Science Organization (ESSO), New Delhi.
  • Mandate: Provide ocean information and advisory services to society, industry, and government.

Role of ESSO

The Earth System Science Organization (ESSO) acts as the executive arm of MoES, implementing policies to address climate change, improve weather predictions, and explore marine resources.

Branches of Earth Sciences
