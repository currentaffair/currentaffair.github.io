India's Space Milestones 2024-2025

Introduction

Launched on December 30, 2024, from Sriharikota, the SpaDeX (Space Docking Experiment) satellites marked a significant achievement in lunar and space station docking.

Key Milestones

  • Successful Docking: On January 16, 2025, SpaDeX satellites achieved in-orbit docking and stabilization, enabling power transfer and undocking protocols.
  • POEM4 Orbital Testbed: Hosted 24 payloads from ISRO, academia, and private industry.

Key Experiments

  • Robotic Arms: Tested debris capture and relocation.
  • Plant Studies: Germination of cowpea seeds (CROPS).
  • Green Propulsion: Tests with MS289 fuel (Manastu Space) and RUDRA engine (Bellatrix Aerospace).
  • Amateur Communications: Successful transmissions from SwetchaSAT-V0 and BGS-ARPIT.
  • Scientific Payloads: GalaxEye's SAR imagery and AI processing by TakeMe2Space.

Indian Space Startups on SpaceX's Transporter 12

  • Pixxel Space: Launched 3 Firefly hyperspectral satellites, forming India's first private satellite constellation.
  • Digantara: Deployed a space camera for tracking low-Earth orbit objects.
  • XDLINX Labs: Delivered a communications satellite for Almagest Space (U.S.).
