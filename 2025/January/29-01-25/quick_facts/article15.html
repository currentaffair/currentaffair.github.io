PM YASASVI Scheme Overview

Introduction

The PM Young Achievers Scholarship Award Scheme for Vibrant India (PM-YASASVI) was addressed to student beneficiaries on Republic Day 2025. It aims to provide financial aid for quality education to marginalized students.

PM-YASASVI Scheme Details

  • Launched by: Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment
  • Purpose: Financial support for quality education for marginalized students
  • Eligibility: Open to OBC, Economically Backward Classes (EBC), and DNT students with family income up to Rs 2.5 lakh per annum.

Sub-Schemes

  • Pre-Matric Scholarship: Rs. 4,000 annual allowance for families with income below Rs. 2.5 lakh.
  • Post-Matric Scholarship: Rs. 5,000 to Rs. 20,000 based on course category.
  • College Education: Full financial support for top college students (tuition, living expenses, education materials).
  • Hostels: Accommodation near government schools and institutions.
