UDISE+ Report 2023-24

The UDISE+ report highlights a decline in India's school enrolment, attributed to revised data collection methods.

Key Findings

Enrolment dropped from 26.36 crore to 25.17 crore in 2022-23, continuing to 24.8 crore in 2023-24. Government schools saw a 5.59% drop, while private schools experienced a 3.67% decline.

Gender and State Trends

Boys' enrolment fell by 6.04% and girls' by 5.76%. Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, and Maharashtra saw the largest declines.

Improved Data Accuracy

Data accuracy improved with Aadhaar-linked student records, removing duplicates from government and private schools.

About UDISE+

UDISE+ is one of the largest Management Information Systems by the Ministry of Education, covering 14.72 lakh schools and 24.8 crore students.
