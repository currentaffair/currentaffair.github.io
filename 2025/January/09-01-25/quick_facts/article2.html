Powerful Earthquake Strikes Tibet, January 9, 2025

Magnitude and Impact

A 7.1 magnitude earthquake struck Tingri County, Tibet, on January 9, 2025, killing 126 people and injuring 188. Tremors were felt in Nepal and parts of India. The mountainous region exacerbated the destruction, with over 3,600 homes collapsed.

Casualties and Damage

The earthquake is the deadliest in China since 2014, with significant damage to infrastructure. Rescue efforts are complicated by harsh weather conditions.

Rescue and Relief Efforts

President Xi Jinping has called for all-out search and rescue efforts. Locals have received essential supplies, and injured individuals have been transferred to hospitals.

Regional Impact

Tremors were also felt in Kathmandu, Nepal, and parts of Bihar, India. Aftershocks followed, complicating rescue operations.
