UDISE+ Report 2023-24

Key Findings

  • Decline in Enrolment: Total enrolment fell by 1.55 crore students from 26.36 crore to 24.8 crore.
  • Government & Private School Decline: Government schools saw a 5.59% drop, while private schools had a 3.67% decline.
  • Gender-wise Trends: Boys' enrolment declined by 6.04%, and girls' enrolment dropped by 5.76%.
  • State-wise Declines: Significant enrolment drops recorded in Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, and Maharashtra.
  • Level-wise Trends: Decline observed across primary, upper primary, and secondary levels, but pre-primary and higher secondary enrolment rose.

Improved Data Accuracy

  • Aadhaar-linked records: Improved data accuracy by removing duplicates of students enrolled in both government and private schools.

About UDISE+

  • Scope: Covers 14.72 lakh schools, 98.08 lakh teachers, and 24.8 crore students.
