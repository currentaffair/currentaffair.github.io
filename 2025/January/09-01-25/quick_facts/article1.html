Indonesia Joined BRICS

Indonesia has officially joined the BRICS group as the 10th member, effective January 2025.

What is BRICS?

BRICS is an informal grouping of emerging economies including Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa. Iran, UAE, Egypt, Ethiopia, and Saudi Arabia became members in 2024.
