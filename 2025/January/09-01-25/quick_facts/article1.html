Border Fencing Dispute: Kaliachak, Malda

Key Points

  • Location: Kaliachak, Malda district, West Bengal.
  • Issue: Ongoing border fencing by BSF despite objections from BGB.
  • Area Affected: Sukdebpur area under Kaliachak 3 block.
  • Involved Parties: BSF (Border Security Force), BGB (Border Guard Bangladesh).
  • Dispute Over Territorial Control: Allegations from BGB on control of Indian land, which BSF denies.
  • Political Context: Political turmoil in Bangladesh resurfaces border fencing issue.
  • Local Reactions: BJP MLA visits to engage with officials and discuss the issue.

Way Forward

  • Strengthen Border Management: Focus on completing fencing and improving surveillance.
  • Diplomatic Engagement: Continued dialogue to resolve tensions and improve coordination.
  • Addressing Misunderstandings: Work towards clarifying territorial disputes and misinformation.
