Indonesia Joined BRICS

Key Points

  • New Member: Indonesia has officially joined BRICS as the 10th member starting January 2025.
  • BRICS Overview: BRICS stands for Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa, with recent expansions including Iran, UAE, Egypt, Ethiopia, and Saudi Arabia in 2024.
  • Economic Influence: BRICS now represents 35% of global GDP and 46% of the world's population.
  • Counterbalance to G7: BRICS aims to challenge the Western-led financial system and promote greater influence for emerging economies.
  • Future Goals: Reduce reliance on the US dollar and promote equity in global institutions like the IMF and UN.

Future Agendas

  • Non-Dollar Transactions: BRICS seeks to strengthen local currencies and reduce the global dependence on the US dollar.
  • Global Reforms: The group advocates for reforms in international organizations to ensure more inclusivity and fairness.
