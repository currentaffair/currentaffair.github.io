30th Anniversary of World Trade Organisation

On 1st January 2025, the World Trade Organization (WTO) celebrated its 30th anniversary, marking three decades of regulating global trade.

About the WTO

The WTO was established under the Marrakesh Agreement on 15th April 1994, succeeding the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT). It covers trade in goods, services, and intellectual property.

Headquarters and Membership

The WTO is based in Geneva, Switzerland, and has 166 member countries, representing 98% of global trade.

Key WTO Agreements

  • TRIMS: Prohibits discriminatory measures against foreign products.
  • TRIPS: Resolves disputes related to intellectual property rights.
  • AoA: Promotes agricultural trade liberalization.
