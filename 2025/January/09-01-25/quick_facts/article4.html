UDISE+ Report 2023-24: Decline in School Enrolment

Key Findings

  • Decline in Enrolment: Enrolment dropped from 26.36 crore to 24.8 crore, a loss of 1.55 crore students.
  • Government & Private School Trends: Govt school enrolment dropped by 5.59%, and private school enrolment by 3.67%.
  • Gender-wise Trends: Boys’ enrolment dropped by 6.04%, while girls’ enrolment fell by 5.76%.
  • State-wise Declines: Major declines in Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, and Maharashtra.
  • Level-wise Trends: Decline across primary, upper primary, and secondary levels, but pre-primary and higher secondary levels showed an increase.

Improved Data Accuracy

The use of Aadhaar-linked student records improved data accuracy by eliminating duplicates.

About UDISE+

The UDISE+ system covers 14.72 lakh schools, 98.08 lakh teachers, and 24.8 crore children, offering valuable data for educational planning and policy-making.
