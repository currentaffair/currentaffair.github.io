New ISRO Chairman Dr. V. Narayanan

Dr. V. Narayanan will succeed S. Somanath as ISRO Chairman and Secretary of the Department of Space (DoS), starting 14th January 2025, for a two-year tenure.

ISRO's Formation and History

ISRO was formed on 15th August 1969, succeeding the Indian National Committee for Space Research (INCOSPAR). The Space Commission and DoS were formed in 1972, bringing ISRO under their control.
