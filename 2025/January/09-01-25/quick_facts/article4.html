New ISRO Chairman Dr. V. Narayanan

Key Points

  • New Chairman: Dr. V. Narayanan will take over as ISRO Chairman and Secretary of the Department of Space (DoS) from 14th January 2025.
  • Upcoming Missions: Narayanan will lead key projects like NVS-02, the Gaganyaan mission, and the NISAR satellite launch in 2025.
  • Contributions of S. Somanath: Led major missions like Chandrayaan-3 and Aditya-L1, while fostering collaboration with private space ventures.

Background on ISRO

  • Formation: ISRO was formed in 1969, following the establishment of INCOSPAR in 1962 under Dr. Vikram Sarabhai.
  • Space Commission: The Space Commission and DoS were set up in 1972 to oversee space activities in India.
  • Headquarters: ISRO's headquarters is located at Antariksh Bhavan, Bangalore.
