BHARATPOL Portal

The Union Home Minister of India launched the 'BHARATPOL' portal to enhance the efficiency of Indian investigation agencies in tracking fugitives and facilitating faster international assistance.

BHARATPOL Portal Overview

Developed by the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI), this portal enables central and state agencies to connect with INTERPOL for real-time information sharing. It replaces the previous system where only the CBI had access to such data.

Enhancing Transnational Crime Prevention

BHARATPOL streamlines international assistance for addressing rising transnational crimes such as cybercrime, drug trafficking, and human trafficking. It also improves the processing of Red Notices and other INTERPOL notices, making it easier for field officers to act swiftly.

Capacity Building

The CBI is tasked with training states on the use of BHARATPOL and implementing new criminal laws for more effective trials.
