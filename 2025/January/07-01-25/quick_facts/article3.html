World Braille Day 2025

World Braille Day, celebrated on 4th January, honors the birth of Louis Braille, the inventor of Braille, a tactile code that allows blind and visually impaired individuals to read and write.

Louis Braille's Early Life

  • Born: 1809 in France, lost his sight at 3 years old.
  • Royal Institute for Blind Youth: At age 10, he was inspired by Charles Barbier's "night writing" system for soldiers to create Braille.
