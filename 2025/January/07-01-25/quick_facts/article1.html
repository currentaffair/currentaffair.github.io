The Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR) celebrated its 40th Foundation Day on 4th January 2025, marking four decades of contributions to Indiaâ€™s scientific and industrial development.

DSIR Overview

  • Established on 4th January 1985 under the Ministry of Science and Technology.
  • Promotes indigenous technology development, utilization, and transfer.
  • Oversees organizations like CSIR, NRDC, and CEL.
  • Supports APCTT under the United Nations Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific (UN-ESCAP).

Key Schemes

  • PRISM: Supports innovators and MSMEs for inclusive development.
  • PACE: Supports industries in patent acquisition and technology development.
  • CTRDH: Provides shared infrastructure and support for MSMEs.
  • A2K+: Promotes dissemination of technology and innovation information.
