Bharat Ranbhoomi Darshan App

Overview

The Bharat Ranbhoomi Darshan app, launched during the 77th Army Day celebrations in Pune, Maharashtra, aims to promote battlefield tourism and foster socio-economic development in border areas.

Key Features

  • Details on battlefields and border areas.
  • Virtual tours, historical stories, and interactive content.
  • Integrated under the Incredible India campaign by the Ministry of Tourism.
  • Trip planning support, including permits for restricted locations.

Highlighted Locations

Key locations include Galwan Valley (Ladakh), Doklam (Bhutan-China-India triangle), and 75 other border areas along the LoC and LAC. Historic sites like the Battle of Longewala (1971 Indo-Pak War) are also featured.

About Doklam

Doklam, near the Chumbi Valley, is contested between Bhutan and China. A key flashpoint for India-China relations, agreements in 1988 and 1998 emphasized maintaining peace and the status quo.
