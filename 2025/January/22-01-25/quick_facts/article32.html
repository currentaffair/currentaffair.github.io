Abd-al-Kuri Island and Yemen Overview

Context: A mysterious airstrip being built on Abd-al-Kuri Island, Yemen, is nearing completion, according to satellite photos analyzed by the Associated Press.

About Abd-al-Kuri Island

Location: 65 miles southwest of Socotra in the Indian Ocean.

Country: Yemen, with mainland Yemen over 320 km to the north.

Population: Fewer than 500 residents, primarily relying on fishing.

Flora: Notable for Euphorbia abdalkuri, a unique and toxic plant.

About Yemen

Geography: Located on the Arabian Peninsula, Yemen borders Saudi Arabia to the north and Oman to the northeast.

Population: Estimated at 34.7 million, mostly Arab Muslims with a rich ethnic diversity.
