Pixxel Launches India's First Private Earth Imaging Satellite Constellation

Context: Pixxel has recently launched India's first private earth imaging satellite constellation, named 'Firefly'.

About Firefly

Firefly is India's first private satellite constellation developed by Pixxel, a Bengaluru-based space-tech startup. It is the world’s highest-resolution hyperspectral satellite constellation, offering detailed data about Earth's surface.

Applications

  • Monitoring deforestation and climate change
  • Early detection of crop diseases
  • Tracking ocean pollution
  • Assessing soil health
  • Enhancing resource management in mining and agriculture

Satellites and Development

The first batch of Firefly satellites was launched via SpaceX's Transporter-12 mission. Pixxel plans to launch three more satellites later this year.

About Pixxel

Founded in 2019 by Awais Ahmed and Kshitij Khandelwal, Pixxel has raised $95 million and is leading India’s commercial satellite development.

Comparison with ISRO

Firefly is India's first commercial satellite constellation, marking a shift in private sector involvement in space, while ISRO operates over 52 satellites for Earth imaging.
