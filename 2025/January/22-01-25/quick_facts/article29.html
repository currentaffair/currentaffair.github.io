Bharathapuzha River Incident and Information

Context: Recently, a family of four drowned in the Bharathapuzha at Painkulam, near Cheruthuruthy.

About Bharathapuzha

Origin: Small streams in various parts of the Western Ghats near the Anamalai range in Tamil Nadu.

Flow Path: Flows west through the Palakkad Gap (Palghat Gap), through the Palakkad and Malappuram districts of Kerala.

Tributaries: Tirur River (major), Gayathripuzha, Kalpathipuzha, and Pulanthode.

Catchment Area: Kerala: 4,400 km², Tamil Nadu: 1,786 km².

Usage: Primarily used for irrigation. Known as the "cultural lifeblood of Kerala."

Rivers of Kerala

Periyar River

Origin: Sivagiri peaks, Peerumedu, Idukki district. Flows southwest through Idukki, Ernakulam, and Thrissur districts, covering ~244 km.

Pamba River

Origin: Pulachimalai Hills, Peerumedu taluk, Idukki district. Flows south through Pathanamthitta and Alappuzha districts.

Chaliyar River

Origin: Elambalari Hills, Nilambur, Kerala. Flows through Malappuram and Kozhikode districts.
