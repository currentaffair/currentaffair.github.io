National Panchayat Awards 2024

The National Panchayat Awards 2024 were presented to 45 Panchayats across India by the President of India for their outstanding contributions to rural governance, inclusive growth, and sustainable development.

Key Facts:

The awards commemorate the enactment of the 73rd Constitutional Amendment Act, 1992, granting constitutional status to Panchayats. They are typically awarded on National Panchayati Raj Day (24th April).

Awards Categories:

The categories include:

  • Deen Dayal Upadhyay Panchayat Satat Vikas Puraskar
  • Nanaji Deshmukh Sarvottam Panchayat Satat Vikas Puraskar
  • Gram Urja Swaraj Vishesh Panchayat Puraskar
  • Carbon Neutral Vishesh Panchayat Puraskar
  • Panchayat Kshamta Nirmaan Sarvottam Sansthan Puraskar

Related Initiatives:

Other initiatives related to Panchayats include:

  • SVAMITVA Scheme: Providing "Record of Rights" for rural households
  • e-Gram Swaraj: Simplified application for financial management
  • Geo-Tagging of Assets: Capturing asset photos with GPS coordinates
  • Citizen Charter: Platform for Panchayats to ensure timely service delivery
