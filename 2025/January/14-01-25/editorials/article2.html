162nd Birth Anniversary of Swami Vivekananda

Introduction

On National Youth Day, which commemorates the 162nd birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda, the Prime Minister participated in the Viksit Bharat Young Leaders Dialogue 2025. This event highlights the significance of youth empowerment, inspired by the teachings of Swami Vivekananda. The National Youth Day, celebrated on January 12th, aims to honor the life and teachings of this great spiritual leader.

What is the Viksit Bharat Young Leaders Dialogue?

The Viksit Bharat Young Leaders Dialogue is a platform for youth involvement in nation-building. It aligns with the Prime Minister’s call for engaging 1 lakh youth in political leadership, free from political affiliations. It involves 3,000 selected youth, aged 15-29 years, who contribute ideas in areas such as technology, sustainability, women empowerment, and more.

Key Facts Regarding Swami Vivekananda

Swami Vivekananda, born as Narendra Nath Datta, was a monk and a key figure in introducing Indian philosophies like Vedanta and Yoga to the world. His teachings emphasized self-realization, the importance of character-building education, and national regeneration.

Contributions of Swami Vivekananda

Swami Vivekananda’s work in spiritual and philosophical circles emphasized selfless service and the importance of a balanced approach to life. He introduced concepts like 'neo-Vedanta', which combined spirituality with material progress.

Core Values and Ethics

Swami Vivekananda’s teachings highlighted youth empowerment, ethical leadership, and rationality. His nationalism was rooted in humanism and universalism, aiming for freedom, equality, and service to humanity.

Important Values: Ethics, education, rationality, and nationalism were central to Vivekananda’s teachings.

Views Related to Vivekananda

Swami Vivekananda was critical of the exploitation of the poor and the masses. He emphasized equality, the dignity of humanity, and service to the less fortunate.

National Youth Policy (NYP) 2014

The National Youth Policy 2014 aimed at empowering India’s youth by focusing on education, employment, and social justice. The draft for NYP 2024 is aligned with SDGs, emphasizing leadership, healthcare, and justice.

Key Areas for NYP 2024: Education, employment, leadership, health, and social justice.

Conclusion

The Viksit Bharat Young Leaders Dialogue, along with Swami Vivekananda’s teachings, encourages youth empowerment and holistic development. These efforts align with national policies like NYP 2024, aiming to create a brighter, sustainable future for India by empowering its youth.
