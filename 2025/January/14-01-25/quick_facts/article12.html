Indian Companies and Data Localisation

Indian companies are capitalising on policy changes to localise data storage and telecom equipment production. Firms are increasingly focusing on manufacturing networking equipment and establishing data storage solutions within India.

Relevance & Key Players

GS 2 (Governance)
Key players: CloudPhotonix and DigiBoxx.

Data Localisation and Regulation

  • The Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 supports future data localisation in certain sectors.
  • The Reserve Bank of India mandates local storage of payment data.
  • Privacy concerns and regulatory compliance are driving the demand for local storage.

Optical Transceiver Market Growth

The global optical transceiver market is projected to reach $47.64 billion by 2035, driven by increased demand for networking and telecom equipment. Local production of transceivers is gaining momentum due to geopolitical tensions.

Challenges and Opportunities

  • Reducing reliance on foreign, especially Chinese, technology.
  • CloudPhotonix targets indigenous networking components crucial for telecom networks and data centres.

Cost and Security Considerations

Local data storage options like DigiBoxx may be more expensive but are gaining traction due to the need for compliance with regulations and rising data privacy concerns.

Industry Support

The Union Secretary for Electronics and Information Technology, S. Krishnan, has welcomed the localisation efforts, which align with national interests and commercial viability.
