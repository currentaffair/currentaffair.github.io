Indian Companies Localizing Telecom and Data Storage Production

Indian companies are taking advantage of policy changes to focus on local data storage and telecom equipment production. Firms are prioritizing the manufacturing of networking equipment and setting up data storage solutions within India.

Key Players

CloudPhotonix: A company founded by telecom transceiver experts, focusing on replacing Chinese-made components with locally produced ones for telecom networks.

DigiBoxx: A cloud storage platform that offers Indian-based servers and data centers, addressing the demand for data localization and regulatory compliance.

Data Localization and Regulation

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act of 2023 opens the possibility of data localization in certain sectors. The Reserve Bank of India mandates payment data storage locally. Local data storage is becoming a priority for firms due to privacy concerns and regulatory compliance, even though it may come at a higher cost.

Optical Transceiver Market Growth

The optical transceiver market is expected to reach $47.64 billion by 2035, fueled by growing demand for telecom and networking equipment. While transceivers have traditionally been imported, local production is increasing.

Challenges and Opportunities

The push for reducing reliance on foreign (particularly Chinese) technology since 2020 has increased the need for local telecom equipment production. CloudPhotonix is working to meet the growing need for indigenous networking components, critical for telecom networks and data centers.

Cost and Security Considerations

Although local storage options like DigiBoxx may be more expensive, they are gaining traction due to the importance of regulatory compliance and data privacy. Local storage is becoming essential for firms to ensure security and protect their digital assets.

Industry Support

S. Krishnan, Union Secretary for Electronics and Information Technology, has welcomed the localization efforts in the telecom and data storage sectors, noting that these efforts are commercially driven and align with national interests.
