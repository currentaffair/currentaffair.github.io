Henley Passport Index 2025

Overview

India's ranking in the Henley Passport Index 2025 has dropped to 85th, with visa-free access to 57 countries. The country has seen fluctuating rankings over the years, from 71st in 2006 to 90th in 2021 due to the Covid-19 pandemic.

Henley Passport Index 2025 Details

The Henley Passport Index ranks 199 global passports based on visa-free access to 227 destinations, with data sourced from the International Air Transport Association (IATA). It is compiled by Henley & Partners, a global citizenship advisory firm.

Key Highlights

  • Top Rankings: Singapore (195 destinations), Japan (193), EU countries and South Korea (192).
  • Falling Rankings: US (2nd to 9th), UK (1st to 5th).
  • Bottom Countries: Pakistan and Yemen (103rd), Iraq, Syria, and Afghanistan.

Passport Costs

As per 2024 data, Mexico, Australia, and the USA have the most expensive passports, while the UAE, India, and Hungary have the cheapest. India's passport offers the best "cost per year" value.
