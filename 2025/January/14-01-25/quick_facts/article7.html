Wildfires in California

Los Angeles is battling devastating wildfires, with fatalities and structural losses. Pink fire retardants are being used to contain the flames.

Causes of Frequent Wildfires

  • Natural Causes: Lightning strikes, climate change, and dry Santa Ana winds.
  • Human Intervention: Campfires, roadside sparks, and faulty power lines.

Impact of Wildfires

  • Economic loss from destruction of life and property.
  • Air pollution with harmful chemicals and dust.
  • Land degradation and loss of biodiversity.

What is Pink Fire Retardant?

Pink fire retardant is a chemical mixture used to slow or extinguish wildfires. It contains ammonium phosphate and toxic metals like chromium and cadmium.

The chemical is visible (pink) for better targeting and helps prevent combustion by limiting oxygen. However, its toxicity poses risks to human health and aquatic life.
