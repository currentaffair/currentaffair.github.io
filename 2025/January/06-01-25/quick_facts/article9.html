Panchayat Se Parliament 2.0 Initiative

Why in News?
The government launched the "Panchayat Se Parliament 2.0" initiative to educate women leaders in local governance about India’s Constitution and parliamentary procedures.

Launch Details

The initiative was inaugurated by Lok Sabha Speaker Om Birla and aims to empower over 500 women representatives from Panchayati Raj Institutions.

Programme Highlights

The initiative includes workshops on legislative processes and democratic institutions. Participants will also visit key sites like the new Parliament House, Pradhanmantri Sangrahalaya, and Rashtrapati Bhavan.

Goal

The primary goal is to enhance the understanding of India’s legislative process and democratic functioning among women leaders in local governance.
