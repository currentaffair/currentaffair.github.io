3 Years of Open Network for Digital Commerce

Why in News?
The Prime Minister of India highlighted the Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) for its transformative impact on e-commerce and empowering small businesses. ONDC recently completed 3 years with over 15 million monthly transactions.

What is ONDC?

Launched in April 2022, ONDC aims to democratize digital commerce through an open, inclusive, and interoperable platform, focusing on cost efficiency, market expansion, and consumer empowerment.

Key Features

ONDC operates on open protocols, allowing seamless transactions across platforms, with participants including buyers, sellers, logistics providers, and tech enablers.

Benefits

ONDC empowers MSMEs by enhancing visibility, lowering operational costs, and supporting women-led and marginalized enterprises. It also offers opportunities for startups to collaborate in logistics, IT, and seller-buyer platforms.

Achievements

ONDC has expanded to over 616 cities, with pilots in Bengaluru, Delhi, and Himachal Pradesh. The platform covers sectors such as food, mobility, fashion, and B2B.

Awards and Recognition

ONDC has received recognition for its application of emerging technologies and was named "Start-up of the Year" in 2024 at the India Digital Awards.
