India-France Defence Deals

Introduction

India and France are finalizing defence deals worth over $10 billion, which include fighter jets and submarines for the Indian Navy.

Details of the Deals

The agreements cover:

  • 26 Rafale-M fighter jets for Indian Navy aircraft carriers
  • Three additional Scorpene-class submarines

Timeline and Approval

These deals are expected to be presented to the Cabinet Committee on Security (CCS) soon, with quick implementation anticipated due to the government-to-government nature of the agreements.

Strategic Context

Prime Minister Narendra Modi is expected to visit Paris in February for the Artificial Intelligence Action Summit, potentially finalizing these deals during the visit.

Expert Insights

The Indian Navy Chief confirmed the deals are near approval, with one last clearance pending for the Rafale-M contract. The Scorpene submarine deal is a continuation of a previous order.
