President Murmu’s Invite for Republic Day Reception

Overview

This year’s Republic Day reception at Rashtrapati Bhavan departs from tradition to highlight India’s heritage, particularly regional crafts from southern India. The initiative is aimed at promoting local artisans and sustainable crafts, celebrating 75 years of the Republic of India.

Themes in the Initiative

The reception highlights crafts from Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Telangana, and Andhra Pradesh, such as Kanjeevaram silk, Etikoppaka dolls, and Kalamkari art. This marks a shift toward regional inclusivity and national pride through cultural representation.

Geographical Indication and Sustainability

The event promotes GI-tagged products and aligns with the “One District One Product” scheme to boost local economies. It also emphasizes sustainability with eco-friendly packaging and the revival of dying crafts.
