Climate-Resilient Agriculture: Comprehensive Approaches

Adaptation Strategies

  • Development of drought- and flood-resistant crops to withstand extreme weather.
  • Efficient water management techniques for optimized usage.
  • Expansion of crop insurance schemes to protect farmer livelihoods during calamities.

Mitigation Strategies

  • Promotion of natural farming to reduce chemical inputs and enhance soil health.
  • Introduction of carbon market incentives for climate-smart farming practices.

Role of PACS

PACS (Primary Agricultural Cooperative Societies) are being expanded to include grain storage, procurement, and more, aiding in rural food security and reducing transportation costs.

NABARD’s Contribution

NABARD’s green lending initiatives and resource mobilization support climate-resilient agricultural activities, with a focus on digitization and bio-energy innovations.

Broader Collaboration and Innovations

  • Addressing irregular monsoons and promoting new seed technologies.
  • Highlighting the role of technology like drones and solar energy for resilience.
