10 Years of NITI Aayog

Overview

On 1st January 2025, NITI Aayog completed 10 years of transforming India since its inception in 2015. It replaced the Planning Commission to adapt to a dynamic economy.

Key Achievements & Contributions

  • Policy Advisory Shift: Focused on decentralized governance and policy guidance.
  • Strengthened Federalism: Through data-driven indices like SDG India Index and Water Management Index.
  • State Institutions of Transformation (SITs): Assisted states in improving governance.
  • Aspirational Blocks Programme: Focused on 500 underdeveloped blocks for government scheme coverage.
  • Atal Innovation Mission (AIM): Trained over one crore students through innovation labs and incubation centres.
  • Initiatives: Conceptualized e-Mobility, Green Hydrogen, and PLI Scheme.
