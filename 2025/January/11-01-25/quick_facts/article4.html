Centre Calls for Comprehensive Approach to Address Impact of Climate Change on Agriculture

Adaptation Strategies

- Development of drought- and flood-resistant crops.
- Efficient water management techniques.
- Expansion of crop insurance schemes to support farmers during natural calamities.

Mitigation Strategies

- Promotion of natural farming to reduce chemical inputs and enhance soil health.
- Introduction of carbon market incentives for climate-smart farming practices.

Role of PACS and NABARD

- PACS to expand beyond credit supply to grain storage and procurement.
- NABARD to focus on green lending, data access, and resource mobilization.

Broader Innovations

- Use of new seeds, bio-energy, digitization, and price risk management.
- Technology initiatives like drone and solar usage to enhance resilience.

Conclusion

A comprehensive approach combining adaptation and mitigation ensures a climate-resilient agricultural sector, safeguarding food security and farmer welfare.
