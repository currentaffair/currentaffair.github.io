President Murmu’s Republic Day Reception Honours Southern Crafts

Emphasizing Cultural Representation

This year’s “At Home” reception highlights the rich craft traditions of southern India, marking a shift toward celebrating Indian heritage and regional diversity. Items from Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Telangana, and Andhra Pradesh showcase a pan-Indian ethos.

Prominent Craft Contributions

  • Tamil Nadu: Kanjeevaram silk pouch
  • Kerala: Screwpine leaf bookmark
  • Karnataka: Ganjifa art fridge magnet
  • Telangana: Ikat-Pochampalli cover
  • Andhra Pradesh: Etikoppaka dolls

Geographical Indications and Local Craft Promotion

Incorporating GI-tagged items aligns with the government’s “One District One Product” scheme, promoting unique local crafts, economic empowerment, and sustainability.

Sustainability and Craft Revival

Reusable packaging and the inclusion of dying crafts, like Ganjifa art, reflect President Murmu’s focus on environmental consciousness and cultural preservation.
