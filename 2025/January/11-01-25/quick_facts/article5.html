GOBARdhan Scheme

Overview

The GOBARdhan initiative focuses on converting waste into wealth by promoting a circular economy. It supports the development of Biogas/Compressed Biogas (CBG)/Bio-CNG plants for sustainable growth.

Key Issues

  • Slow Adoption: Only 115 CBG plants are operational, far behind the target of 5,000 by 2030.
  • Information Gaps: Lack of details about feedstocks used by specific CBG plants on the GOBARdhan portal.
  • Operational Transparency: Missing section for operational plants with updated data, hindering policy actions and solutions for entrepreneurs.

What is CBG?

Compressed Biogas (CBG) is a renewable energy source made from organic waste like agricultural residues, cattle dung, municipal solid waste, and sewage sludge. It helps in reducing fossil fuel usage, managing agricultural waste, and mitigating open burning.
