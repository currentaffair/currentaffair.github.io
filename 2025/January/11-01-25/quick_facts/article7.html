India-US Initiatives in Defence and Nuclear Cooperation

Why in News?

The US National Security Advisor visited India and signed new initiatives in technology and defence cooperation.

New Initiatives Signed

  • Civil Nuclear Cooperation: The US will lift restrictions to provide nuclear reactors to Indian entities like BARC.
  • Sonobuoy Co-Manufacturing: Enhances the Indian Navy's capabilities in detecting submarines and underwater threats.
  • Missile Export Control: Updates to missile export controls for space cooperation, including collaboration on emerging technologies.
  • iCET Advancement: Both nations reaffirmed cooperation in AI, quantum computing, telecommunications, and space.

What are Sonobuoys?

  • Expendable acoustic sensors used to detect and track underwater sounds from ships and submarines.
  • They are activated upon hitting water and communicate with the tracking ship or aircraft for about 24 hours.

India-US Civil Nuclear Cooperation Agreement

  • The 123 Agreement enables India to access nuclear fuel, technology, and reactors for peaceful purposes.
  • India has agreed to place its civilian nuclear facilities under IAEA safeguards to ensure peaceful use.
  • The US seeks an NSG exemption to facilitate trade with India's expanding peaceful nuclear sector.
