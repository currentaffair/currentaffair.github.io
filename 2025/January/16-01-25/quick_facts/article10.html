Inflation Trends in India - December 2024

Retail Inflation

India's retail inflation moderated to 5.22% in December 2024, the lowest in four months, down from 5.48% in November. Food inflation decreased to 8.39%, while core inflation fell to 3.6%, indicating subdued demand conditions.

Sector-Specific Inflation

Food & beverages inflation decreased from 8.20% in November to 7.69% in December. Inflation for perishables like vegetables and pulses also eased, while oils and fats saw a rise of 14.6%, signaling concerns.

The ongoing strong rabi sowing is expected to cool food inflation further. Despite recent easing, food inflation remains a major challenge for the economy.

Future Outlook

Analysts foresee a cautious RBI approach to rate cuts, balancing inflation control with broader economic stability. Elevated food prices and global economic uncertainty could delay inflation reaching the target range.
