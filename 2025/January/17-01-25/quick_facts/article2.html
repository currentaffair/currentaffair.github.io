Data Embassies

Overview

India is advancing discussions to host UAE's first data embassy, with the inaugural setup potentially located in Andhra Pradesh. These embassies will allow countries to store copies of their sovereign data while maintaining control over it.

Purpose and Benefits

  • Ensures data continuity during natural disasters or geopolitical crises.
  • Special strategic zones in India will house these data centers.
  • Embassies will operate like consular divisions, managing privacy, access, and data handling.

Global Context

Estonia pioneered the concept by storing its citizens' digitized data in Luxembourg after a 2007 cyberattack. Data embassies enable localization without adhering to foreign data regulations.

Significance

This initiative enhances India's geopolitical standing as a reliable and stable region for sovereign data storage amid global uncertainties.
