About Gangasagar Mela

The Gangasagar Mela is an annual religious festival held on Sagar Island, West Bengal, at the confluence of the Ganga and Bay of Bengal.

It coincides with Makar Sankranti and involves rituals like taking a holy dip in the Ganga and offering "Arghya" to the Sun God for spiritual upliftment.

The festival is the second-largest religious gathering in India after the Kumbh Mela and has historical ties to ancient texts such as the Mahabharata.
