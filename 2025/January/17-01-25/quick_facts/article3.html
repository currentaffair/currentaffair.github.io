150th Foundation Day of IMD and Mission Mausam

Why in News? The Prime Minister of India participated in the 150th Foundation Day celebrations of the Indian Meteorological Department (IMD) on January 15th, 2025, at New Delhi. The Mission Mausam initiative was also launched, alongside the IMD Vision-2047 document release.

Mission Mausam Initiative

The Mission Mausam initiative aims to enhance India's weather forecasting capabilities. It has a Rs 2,000 crore budget for its first two years, focusing on upgrading weather forecasting, improving forecasting accuracy, and deploying advanced technologies like AI and machine learning.

IMD Vision-2047 Document

The Vision-2047 document outlines strategic targets for the IMD by 2047, including 100% detection of severe weather events and zero deaths from weather-related events. It aims to achieve significant improvements in forecasting accuracy over the coming decades.

India Meteorological Department (IMD)

IMD is responsible for meteorological observations, weather forecasting, and seismology in India. It was established in 1875 and operates under the Ministry of Earth Sciences. IMD also conducts research to improve meteorological science.
