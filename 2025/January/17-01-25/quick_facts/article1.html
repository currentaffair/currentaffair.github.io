Israel and Hamas Ceasefire Agreement

Ceasefire Terms

Duration: Initial six-week halt to hostilities.

Hostage Exchange: Hamas will release 33 hostages, Israel will release Palestinian prisoners.

Humanitarian Aid: Aid to flow into Gaza and displaced Palestinians may return home.

Humanitarian Impact

Over 46,000 Palestinians killed, 90% of Gaza's population displaced, and significant destruction of homes and hospitals.

Political Repercussions

The Israeli government faces domestic pressures while Hamas's hostages release raises questions about leverage.

Future of Gaza

The governance and reconstruction of Gaza remain unresolved, with the potential for international involvement.

Challenges Ahead

Ceasefire Implementation: Ensuring both sides adhere to terms.

Reconstruction: Effective aid distribution and rebuilding amidst political and logistical hurdles.
