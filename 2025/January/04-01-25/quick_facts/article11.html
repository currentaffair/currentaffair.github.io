Extension of Crop Insurance Schemes

Why in News?

The Union Cabinet has approved measures to support Indian farmers, including the extension of a special subsidy for Diammonium Phosphate (DAP) fertilizers and continuation of crop insurance schemes until 2025-26.

Recent Key Measures

  • Continuation of Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana and RWBCIS until 2025-26.
  • Extension of the special DAP package until 2025, ensuring affordable fertilizers.
  • Creation of a Fund for Innovation and Technology (FIAT) with a Rs 824.77 crore corpus.
  • YES-TECH and WINDS initiatives for technological advancements in yield estimation and weather data collection.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

A crop insurance scheme protecting farmers from financial losses due to crop failures caused by natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

Premium rates: 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, and 5% for commercial horticulture crops.
