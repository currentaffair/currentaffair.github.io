Delhi Schools Digitalisation

Overview

Delhi’s digital infrastructure in schools has significantly outpaced the national average, as shown by the Ministry of Education’s UDISE+ data.

Delhi's Digital Edge

  • 99.1% of Delhi schools have functional desktops (National: 32.4%)
  • 60.9% of Delhi schools have projectors (National: 20.3%)
  • 37.3% of Delhi schools have integrated teaching devices (National: 8.1%)
  • 74% of Delhi schools have smart classrooms (National: 24.4%)

Digital Divide Across States

  • Assam, Bihar, Jharkhand have fewer schools with desktops.
  • Chandigarh leads in integrated teaching devices, while Meghalaya ranks lowest.

Private vs. Government Schools

  • Private schools have more access to mobile phones (50.6%) and smart classrooms (79.5%) compared to government schools (30.2% and 70% respectively).

Impact of the Pandemic

The pandemic accelerated digital teaching, but access to devices remains a challenge, with 30-35% of students unable to attend online classes during closures.

Challenges in Government Schools

Despite improvements, many government school students still lack personal devices for online classes.
