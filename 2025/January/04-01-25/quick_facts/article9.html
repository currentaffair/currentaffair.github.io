Thanthai Periyar Memorial

Overview

The Thanthai Periyar Memorial in Vaikom, Kerala, was recently renovated and inaugurated by the Chief Ministers of Kerala and Tamil Nadu to honor the contributions of E.V. Ramasami Naicker, known as Thanthai Periyar.

Periyar's Contribution

  • Periyar played a key role in the Vaikom Satyagraha (1924), the first organized movement for the rights of untouchable communities in India.
  • He was imprisoned twice for his activism and earned the title 'Vaikom Veeran.'

Key Movements

  • Vaikom Satyagraha (1924-1925) was a peaceful protest for the rights of the untouchables.
  • Periyar founded the Self-Respect Movement and Dravidar Kazhagam and is called the 'Father of the Dravidian movement.'
