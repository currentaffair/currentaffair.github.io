MoU Between CENJOWS and NDMA

Overview

The Centre for Joint Warfare Studies (CENJOWS) signed a MoU with the National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA) to enhance India’s disaster management capabilities.

Objectives of the MoU

  • Address challenges in disaster mitigation, preparedness, and response.
  • Adopt a ‘whole of the nation’ approach to disaster management.

Framework for Joint Initiatives

  • Collaborative Research: Joint studies on disaster management and humanitarian assistance.
  • Capacity Building: Seminars, workshops, and training programs for stakeholders.
  • Awareness Campaigns: Promoting disaster preparedness and risk mitigation.
  • International Engagements: Facilitating global collaboration in disaster management.

About CENJOWS

Established by the Ministry of Defence in 2004, CENJOWS promotes jointness and offers research-based alternatives for policy formulation.

About NDMA

NDMA, established in 2006, is India’s apex statutory body for disaster management. It is chaired by the Prime Minister and includes nine members.
