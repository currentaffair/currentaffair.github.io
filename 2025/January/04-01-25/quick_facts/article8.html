National Sports Awards 2024

Why in News?

The Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports recently announced the National Sports Awards 2024, recognizing outstanding sports personalities and organizations.

Awardees

  • Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award: Gukesh D (Chess), Harmanpreet Singh (Hockey), Praveen Kumar (Para-Athletics), Manu Bhaker (Shooting).
  • Arjuna Awards: Jyothi Yarraji (Athletics), Annu Rani (Athletics), Nitu (Boxing), Rakesh Kumar (Para-Archery), Navdeep (Para-Athletics).
  • Lifetime Arjuna Awards: Sucha Singh (Athletics), Murlikant Rajaram Petkar (Para-Swimming).
  • Dronacharya Award: Subhash Rana (Para-Shooting), S Muralidharan (Badminton).
  • Rashtriya Khel Protsahan Puraskar: Physical Education Foundation of India.
  • MAKA Trophy: Chandigarh University (Overall Winner).

Types of Sports Awards in India

  • Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award: India’s highest sporting honor for outstanding performance over four years.
  • Arjuna Award: For consistent good performance over four years.
  • Dronacharya Award: India’s highest honor for coaches.
  • Major Dhyan Chand Award: Lifetime achievement in sports.
  • MAKA Trophy: Awarded to an institution for performance in inter-university tournaments.
  • Rashtriya Khel Protsahan Puraskar: Awarded to individuals and organizations for promoting sports.
