Key Developments: Wildfires in California

Why in News?

At least 2 fatalities have been reported, and 70,000 residents have been ordered to evacuate due to massive wildfires in California.

Affected Areas

  • Pacific Palisades: Over 5,000 acres burned, with 1,000+ structures destroyed.
  • Eaton Fire: 2,000 acres burned near Pasadena, resulting in 2 fatalities.
  • Hurst Fire: 500 acres burned in Sylmar, northwest of Los Angeles.

Challenges in Firefighting Efforts

Fierce winds hinder firefighting efforts, and all three fires remain 0% contained. Significant injuries have been reported among residents who ignored evacuation orders.

Government Response

California Governor Gavin Newsom declared a state of emergency. President Joe Biden is expected to visit a fire station for a briefing from fire officials. Emergency responders are conducting door-to-door evacuations.

Impact on Residents

Evacuations have caused traffic chaos, with some residents abandoning vehicles. Gusty winds persist, keeping residents on high alert.
