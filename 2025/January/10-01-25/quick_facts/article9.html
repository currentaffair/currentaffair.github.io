Freshwater Species in Crisis

Key Findings

4,294 species are threatened according to the IUCN Red List. There have been 89 confirmed extinctions and 178 suspected extinctions since 1500.

  • Climate change

Impact on Ecosystems

Freshwater ecosystems cover less than 1% of Earth's surface but support 10% of all species globally, including one-third of vertebrates and half of all fish species.

Takeaway

Urgent, targeted actions are required to protect freshwater biodiversity. Integrated water management and species-specific conservation are crucial to prevent further loss.
