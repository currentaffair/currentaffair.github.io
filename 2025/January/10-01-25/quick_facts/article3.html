Genome India Project

Key Features

The Genome India project, housing 10,000 human genomes, aims to map India's genetic diversity to advance precision medicine and improve disease treatments. The database includes 83 population groups and 27 million genetic variants.

Potential Applications

This initiative will contribute to disease research, precision medicine, and drug development by tailoring treatments based on genetic makeup, improving drug efficacy and safety.

Ethical and Privacy Considerations

To protect privacy, data will be coded numerically, and access to it will be controlled by an independent panel to ensure ethical research practices.

Genome India holds the potential to transform healthcare and biotechnology by providing a genetic blueprint for targeted clinical interventions and fostering global health research.
