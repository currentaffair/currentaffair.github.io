Watershed Development Component 2.0 of PMKSY

Why in News? The Ministry of Rural Development has sanctioned 56 new Watershed Development Projects under PMKSY-WDC 2.0, covering 2.8 lakh hectares in 10 states.

What is PMKSY-WDC 2.0?

PMKSY-WDC 2.0 is aimed at improving water and soil resources by enhancing the productivity of rainfed and degraded land. Launched for the period 2021-2026, it seeks to rejuvenate spring sheds and encourage water productivity.

Key Objectives

  • Enhance rainfed and degraded land productivity
  • Strengthen community institutions for livelihoods
  • Promote cross-learning and incentives for watershed projects

Coverage & Approach

The scheme targets 49.50 lakh hectares by 2026, focusing on water productivity and promoting integrated farming systems like horticulture, fisheries, and animal husbandry.

What is PMKSY?

Launched in 2015-16, PMKSY is a national initiative for water conservation, promoting sustainable water management and expansion of irrigation facilities. It consists of multiple components, including AIBP, HKKP, PDMC, and WDC.
