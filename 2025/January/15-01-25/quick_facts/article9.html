Watershed Development Component 2.0 of PMKSY

Key Highlights

The Ministry of Rural Development has sanctioned 56 new watershed development projects under PMKSY-WDC 2.0, covering 2.8 lakh hectares of degraded land across 10 states.

About PMKSY-WDC 2.0

This initiative, launched for 2021-2026, focuses on enhancing rainfed land productivity, community livelihood, and watershed sustainability. It introduces new activities like rejuvenating springsheds.

Next-Generation Focus

Emphasis on water productivity, biological measures, crop diversification, and integrated farming systems such as horticulture, fisheries, and apiculture.

What is PMKSY?

Launched in 2015-16, PMKSY is a centrally sponsored scheme aiming to improve water access for farming, expand irrigated areas, and promote sustainable water conservation.
