India’s Humanitarian Assistance to Cuba

India's Aid to Cuba

India has sent humanitarian aid to Cuba following Hurricane Rafael, aligning with its global solidarity philosophy of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam (the world is one family).

Historical Relations

India was one of the first to recognize Cuba's government post-revolution in 1959. Both nations have supported each other in international forums like the UN and NAM.

Economic and Cultural Ties

India's exports to Cuba in FY 2022-23 included pharmaceuticals and textiles. Both countries also share strong cultural relations, with events promoting Yoga and Ayurveda in Cuba.

India's Assistance

India has provided aid after hurricanes, set up IT centres, and extended LoCs for agriculture and renewable energy sectors.
