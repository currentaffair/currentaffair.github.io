Indian Fishermen Detained by British Navy

15 Indian fishermen were detained by the British Navy near Diego Garcia for allegedly crossing the maritime boundary. This is part of a broader issue where Tamil Nadu fishermen are frequently detained by both the Sri Lankan and British navies, affecting their livelihood and safety.

About Diego Garcia

Diego Garcia is the largest island of the Chagos Archipelago in the Indian Ocean. It was separated from Mauritius in 1965 to create the British Indian Ocean Territory (BIOT) and is administered by Britain, leased to the US for military purposes since 1966.

The island became a key military base in 1986, playing a significant role in the US war on terror operations after the September 11 attacks in 2001. In 2024, Britain agreed to cede sovereignty of the Chagos Archipelago to Mauritius but will retain sovereign rights over Diego Garcia.
