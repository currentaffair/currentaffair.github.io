Cyclone Dikeledi Hits Mayotte

About Mayotte

Mayotte, a French territory in the Indian Ocean, consists of two islands: Grande Terre (Main Island) and Petite Terre (Pamandzi). It is the poorest territory in France and the EU. France colonized Mayotte in 1843 and annexed the Comoros archipelago in 1904. Despite the Comoros declaring independence in 1975, Mayotte remains under French control.

Recent Cyclone Impact

The region was recently hit by Cyclone Dikeledi. This storm follows Cyclone Chido, which had struck Mayotte in December 2024, marking it as the worst storm in 90 years.
