Watershed Development Component 2.0 of PMKSY

Why in News?
The Ministry of Rural Development has sanctioned 56 new Watershed Development Projects under the Watershed Development Component 2.0 (PMKSY-WDC 2.0) in 10 states, covering around 2.8 lakh hectares of degraded land.

What is the Watershed Development Component of PMKSY 2.0?

PMKSY-WDC 2.0 is a component aimed at conserving water and soil resources. The scheme focuses on enhancing rainfed and degraded land productivity, strengthening community institutions, and boosting watershed project efficiency. The initiative targets covering 49.50 lakh hectares of degraded land from 2021 to 2026.

Key Objectives

  • Enhance land productivity through integrated watershed management.
  • Promote crop diversification and integrated farming systems.

What is PMKSY?

PMKSY is a government initiative launched to improve water access for farming, expand irrigated areas, and promote water conservation. It includes components like Accelerated Irrigation Benefits Programme (AIBP) and Per Drop More Crop (PDMC), with the Watershed Development component led by the Ministry of Rural Development.
