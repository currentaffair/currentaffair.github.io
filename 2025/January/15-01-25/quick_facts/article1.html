Commissioning of Nilgiri, Surat and Vaghsheer

Nilgiri: The Lead Ship of Project 17A

Nilgiri, the lead ship of Project 17A, is a major upgrade from the Shivalik-class frigates. It features advanced stealth technology and state-of-the-art weapon systems designed to strengthen naval defense.

Surat: Enhanced Destroyer under Project 15B

Surat is the fourth and final destroyer under Project 15B, an enhanced version of the Kolkata-class destroyers. It is equipped with long-range missiles and indigenous weapons systems, reinforcing the Indian Navy's capabilities.

Vaghsheer: The Scorpene-Class Submarine

Vaghsheer is the sixth submarine of the Scorpene-class (Project 75) and is known for its quiet operation. It is capable of anti-surface and anti-submarine warfare, surveillance, and special operations.
