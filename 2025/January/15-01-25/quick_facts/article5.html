Commissioning of Nilgiri, Surat, and Vaghsheer

Overview of New Naval Assets

The Indian Navy has announced the commissioning of Nilgiri, Surat, and Vaghsheer, which were built at Mazagon Dock Shipbuilders Limited (MDL) on January 15th, 2025, in Mumbai.

Nilgiri

Nilgiri is the lead ship of Project 17A, offering a major upgrade over the Shivalik-class frigates, featuring advanced stealth technology and cutting-edge weapon systems to strengthen India's naval defense.

Surat

Surat is the fourth and final destroyer under Project 15B, an enhanced version of Kolkata-class destroyers, equipped with long-range missiles and indigenous weapons systems.

Vaghsheer

Vaghsheer is the sixth submarine in the Scorpene-class (Project 75). It is one of the quietest diesel-electric submarines globally and is equipped for anti-surface and anti-submarine warfare, surveillance, and special operations.
