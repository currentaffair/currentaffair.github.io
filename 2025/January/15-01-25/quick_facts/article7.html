World Hindi Day 2025

About World Hindi Day

Vishwa Hindi Divas, or World Hindi Day, is celebrated annually on 10th January to promote Hindi's global presence. This day marks the first time Hindi was spoken at the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) in 1949.

Significance

With over 600 million speakers, Hindi is the third most spoken language in the world and the most spoken language in India. The day raises awareness of its importance as a global language of communication.

World Hindi Day 2025 Theme

The theme for World Hindi Day 2025 is "A Global Voice of Unity and Cultural Pride," focusing on promoting linguistic exchange and fostering cultural pride.

Constitutional Status of Hindi

Hindi is the official language of India under Article 343 of the Constitution, alongside English, and is part of the 8th Schedule with 22 languages recognized for official use.
