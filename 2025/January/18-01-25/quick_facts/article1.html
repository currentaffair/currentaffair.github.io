Singapore-India Semiconductor Collaboration

Singapore and India are collaborating to establish a semiconductor ecosystem in India. This includes advanced manufacturing and the development of technological solutions for semiconductors.

Strategic Cooperation Areas

The collaboration includes semiconductor manufacturing, a digital data corridor, and renewable energy projects in eastern India, focusing on Odisha and Assam. Additionally, infrastructure development and logistics strengthening are key aspects.
