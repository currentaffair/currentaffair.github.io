Tamil Nadu Women Drone Operators Initiative

Tamil Nadu has launched its first batch of women drone operators under the Namo Drone Didi scheme, empowering rural women to join the agricultural supply chain.

Program Highlights

The initiative trains women to operate drones for agricultural tasks, reducing time and labor in farming. The program provides drone equipment and training for free.

Challenges and Opportunities

Though there are challenges such as battery limitations and logistics, the program offers economic opportunities for rural women and enhances farming efficiency.
