SANJAY Battlefield Surveillance System

System Overview

SANJAY is an automated battlefield surveillance system designed to integrate data from ground and air sensors, ensuring secure and accurate data processing through army and satellite networks.

Functions

The system monitors vast land boundaries, prevents intrusions, and evaluates situations with high accuracy, serving as a force multiplier for intelligence, supervision, and reconnaissance operations.

Operational Utility

SANJAY enhances commanders' ability to operate effectively in both conventional and sub-conventional scenarios within a networked environment.

Deployment Phases

To be deployed across all Indian Army operational brigades, divisions, and corps by March-October 2025 as part of the Ministry of Defense's "Reform Year" initiative.

Development

The system is jointly developed by the Indian Army and Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL), marking a significant step in indigenous defense technology.

Significance

SANJAY enhances battlefield transparency and revolutionizes battlefield operations through a centralized web application that supports command and decision-making systems.

About Bharat Electronics Limited

Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) is a government-owned aerospace and defense electronics company, founded in 1954 in Bangalore. It manufactures advanced electronics for ground and aerospace applications and has been granted Navratna status by the Government of India.
