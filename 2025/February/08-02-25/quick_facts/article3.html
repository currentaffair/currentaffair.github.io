US Deportation of Indians

Key Event

For the first time, a U.S. military C-17 aircraft was used to deport Indian nationals, as ordered by former U.S. President Donald Trump. The deportees were shackled and handcuffed, sparking outrage in India.

U.S. Justification

U.S. Embassy spokesperson Chris Elms defended the move, citing national security. U.S. Border Patrol chief Michael W. Banks released a video showcasing the deportation to deter illegal migration.

Global Implications

Countries like Guatemala, Peru, Honduras, Ecuador, and Colombia have also seen military deportations. Colombian President Gustavo Pedro criticized it as "undignified."
