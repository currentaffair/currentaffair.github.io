National Manufacturing Mission

Overview

The Union Finance Minister announced the National Manufacturing Mission in the Union Budget 2025-26. It aims to support industries of all sizes under the "Make in India" initiative with policy support, execution roadmaps, and a governance framework for central ministries and states.

Five Focal Areas

  • Ease and cost of doing business
  • Future-ready workforce for in-demand jobs
  • Vibrant and dynamic MSME sector
  • Availability of technology
  • Production of quality products

Support for Clean Tech Manufacturing

The mission will foster Clean Tech manufacturing to boost domestic value in areas like:

  • Solar PV cells
  • EV batteries, motors, and controllers
  • Electrolyzers and wind turbines
  • High voltage transmission equipment
  • Grid-scale batteries

Labor-Intensive Sectors

Specific policies will promote employment and entrepreneurship in labor-intensive sectors.

Focus Product Scheme: Footwear & Leather

The Focus Product Scheme will enhance productivity and competitiveness in the footwear and leather sectors by supporting:

  • Design capacity and component manufacturing
  • Non-leather and leather product machinery

Expected Outcomes: Employment for 22 lakh people, Rs. 4 lakh crore turnover, and Rs. 1.1 lakh crore in exports.

National Action Plan for Toys

The plan aims to make India a global hub for toy manufacturing, focusing on cluster development, skill enhancement, and a robust manufacturing ecosystem for high-quality, sustainable toys.

MSME Enhancements

  • Credit guarantee cover increased from Rs. 5 crore to Rs. 10 crore.
  • Investment and turnover limits for MSMEs raised by 2.5 and 2 times, respectively.
