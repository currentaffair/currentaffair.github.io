India's Space Industrialization

Context

The Indian space sector's recent successes are paving the way for space industrialization and expanding private sector participation.

Growing Capabilities

With the success of Chandrayaan-3, India became the fourth country to achieve a soft landing on the Moon, showcasing its cost-effective space missions.

India launched Aditya-L1, its first solar observatory, and is preparing for the Gaganyaan mission to send Indian astronauts to space by 2025.

Promoting Private Sector Participation

The government introduced the Indian Space Policy 2023, permitting 100% FDI in satellite manufacturing and ground infrastructure.

IN-SPACe was established as a single-window regulator to streamline private sector approvals.
