GARBH-INi-DRISHTI Launch

Context

India has launched the GARBH-INi-DRISHTI data repository to advance biomedical research.

About GARBH-INi

GARBH-INi stands for Interdisciplinary Group for Advanced Research on Birth Outcomes – DBT India Initiative. This collaborative research program, under the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), focuses on improving maternal and child health by understanding pregnancy complications and developing prediction tools for preterm birth.

The program is led by the Translational Health Science and Technology Institute (THSTI) in the NCR Biotech Cluster, Faridabad, and collaborates with multiple research institutions and hospitals across India.

It is part of the Atal Jai Anusandhan Biotech Mission – UNaTI, which promotes research on nationally significant health challenges.

About GARBHINI-DRISHTI

GARBHINI-DRISHTI is a data repository and information-sharing platform under the GARBH-INi program. It provides access to clinical data, images, and biospecimens from over 12,000 pregnant women, newborns, and postpartum mothers.

As one of South Asia’s largest pregnancy cohort datasets, it is a valuable resource for researchers, with clear guidelines ensuring data security, ethical use, and impactful research.

How GARBHINI-DRISHTI Helps Researchers

  • Analyze pregnancy trends and complications.
  • Develop better diagnostic and treatment strategies.
  • Create prediction models for birth outcomes.
  • Collaborate with leading research institutions and hospitals.
