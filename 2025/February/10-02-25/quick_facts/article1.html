Job Types for Viksit Bharat

To achieve the Viksit Bharat vision, India must focus on:

  • Climate-resilient jobs
  • AI-resilient jobs
  • Aspiration-centric jobs

These sectors will help tackle climate change, automation, and evolving youth aspirations while promoting sustainable economic growth.

Importance of Climate-Resilient Jobs

India is highly vulnerable to climate change. In 2019, it was the seventh most-affected country, with an income loss of $159 billion in 2021. By 2030, adaptation costs could soar, highlighting the need for robust climate-resilient job creation.
