Aero India 2025

Introduction

Aero India 2025, hosted at the Indian Air Force station in Yelahanka, Bengaluru from February 10-14, is not merely an exhibition but a dynamic platform fostering

innovation, strategic alliances, and technological advancement
. As the 15th edition, it aims to redefine aerospace expositions, positioning India as a significant global player in aviation.

Under Prime Minister Narendra Modi's leadership, this event has become a global hub for

aerospace innovation, collaboration, and strategic partnerships
. The theme, ‘The Runway to a Billion Opportunities’, emphasizes India's commitment to cutting-edge defence technology and international cooperation.

Transformation Since the 1990s

Since its modest inception in 1996, Aero India has evolved into a

premier global defence event
. This transformation reflects India's significant advancements in aerospace and defence manufacturing capabilities.

Aero India 2025 will feature 106 original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), including 55 foreign OEMs from 19 countries such as Brazil, France, Israel, Japan, Russia, South Korea, the UK, and the USA.

Sixteen Defence Public Sector Undertakings (DPSUs) and 35 Indian OEMs, including Larsen & Toubro, Bharat Forge, and Adani Defence & Aerospace, will showcase their innovations
.

Highlights of the Exposition

For the first time, Aero India will feature two of the world's most advanced fighter jets: the Russian

Su-57
and the American
F-35
. These aircraft represent the pinnacle of stealth, agility, and technological superiority.

India will also display a model of its indigenous

Advanced Medium Combat Aircraft (AMCA)
, a twin-engine fifth-generation stealth fighter with 75% domestic content. This highlights India's progress towards becoming a self-reliant defence manufacturer.

'

Manthan
', a new platform at the event, will promote start-ups and innovation in aerospace and defence, reflecting the government's focus on fostering home-grown solutions through the iDEX (Innovations for Defence Excellence) pavilion.

Visitor Experience

Over 700,000 visitors are expected, with significant enhancements to ensure a seamless experience:

  • Optimized traffic flow with expanded roads and real-time monitoring.
  • Enhanced transport services, including comfortable shuttle options.
  • Robust multi-tiered security with advanced surveillance.
  • Comfort-driven amenities such as spacious halls, increased seating, and improved dining facilities.
  • A specialized
    Aero India 2025 app
    for updates, schedules, and navigation assistance.

Visitors will enjoy thrilling aerial displays and gain insights through seminars, live technology demonstrations, and interactive sessions on the latest advancements in aerospace and defence.

Conclusion

Aero India 2025 is a definitive statement of India's ambition to emerge as a

global leader in defence and aerospace production
. As the nation strides towards greater self-reliance, this event will be a catalyst for bridging technological gaps, fostering innovation, and strengthening international partnerships.
