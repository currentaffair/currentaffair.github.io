International Best Practices in Pension Systems

Sweden

Guaranteed minimum pension regardless of market performance, combining state contributions with individual savings.

United Kingdom (UK)

The National Employment Savings Trust (NEST) provides universal pension coverage through automatic enrollment of eligible workers.

Netherlands

Known for strong regulatory oversight of pension funds, ensuring sustainable payouts and financial security.

Singapore

The Central Provident Fund (CPF) offers tax incentives for voluntary savings, reducing the state pension burden while encouraging retirement savings.

Way Forward

Introduction of a Minimum Guaranteed Pension

Ensuring stable income for retirees, especially during economic crises, while reducing dependence on market-driven returns.

Inclusion of Informal Sector Workers

Broadening UPS to cover workers outside government employment would enhance financial security for a larger population.

Flexible Tenure Requirements

Restructuring the 25-year service rule to progressively reward employees based on years served would accommodate late workforce entrants and career interruptions.

Establishing a Strong Regulatory Framework

Creating an independent Pension Regulatory Authority would ensure transparent fund management and effective oversight.

Enhancing Financial Awareness and Advisory Support

Providing financial literacy programs would help employees make informed decisions regarding their pensions.
