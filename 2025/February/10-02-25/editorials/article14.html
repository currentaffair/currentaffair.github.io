Way Forward

Judicial Clarity on Article 282

The Supreme Court should examine whether CSS impacts the federal balance and define conditions for discretionary grants.

Rationalisation of CSS

Merge similar schemes into umbrella programs and conduct regular impact assessments to eliminate ineffective schemes.

Review Funding Mechanism

Revise fund-sharing patterns to reduce states' financial burdens, particularly for general category states, and reinstate support for backward regions after the discontinuation of the Backward Regions Grant Fund (BRGF).

Strengthening Cooperative Federalism

Establish regular Centre-State consultations via the Inter-State Council and NITI Aayog, granting states greater flexibility in adapting CSS to local contexts.

Conclusion

The evolution of CSS and its growing influence over state fiscal autonomy underscores the need for a balanced approach. By fostering cooperative federalism and ensuring fair financial distribution, India can uphold the constitutional principles of federal governance while addressing developmental priorities across states.
