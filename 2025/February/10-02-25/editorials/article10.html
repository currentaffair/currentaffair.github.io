Aero India 2025

Introduction

Aero India 2025, scheduled from February 10-14 at Yelahanka Air Force Station in Bengaluru, is a premier aerospace and defence exhibition. The 15th edition aims to elevate India’s global standing in aerospace innovation and self-reliance in defence production.

The theme for this year’s event is “The Runway to a Billion Opportunities,” emphasizing India’s commitment to fostering innovation and international collaboration.

Evolution of Aero India: From 1996 to 2025

Started in 1996 as a modest aviation exposition, Aero India has transformed into a globally recognized aerospace and defence event. Over the years, it has attracted leading defence manufacturers, policymakers, and industry leaders.

The 2025 edition is expected to surpass previous milestones in defence production and technological advancements, solidifying India’s position in the global aerospace arena.

Participation & Key Exhibitors

Global Presence

The exhibition will feature

106 Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs)
, including 55 foreign OEMs from 19 countries such as Brazil, France, Israel, Japan, Russia, the UK, and the USA.

Additionally, 16 Defence Public Sector Undertakings (DPSUs) will showcase India’s indigenous capabilities.

Indian Industry Participation

35 Indian OEMs will participate, including major players like Larsen & Toubro, Adani Defence, Mahindra Defence, BrahMos Aerospace, BHEL, and ECIL. This reflects the strong public-private collaboration in defence manufacturing.

Key Highlights of Aero India 2025

Showcasing Advanced Fighter Jets

The event will feature the Russian

Su-57
, a next-generation stealth fighter known for agility and stealth, alongside the American
F-35
, a technologically advanced multi-role combat aircraft. This marks the first time both aircraft will be showcased together, enhancing India’s position in global defence diplomacy.

Indigenous Fifth Generation Fighter Aircraft – AMCA

The

Advanced Medium Combat Aircraft (AMCA)
will be showcased with 75% indigenous content. This development marks India’s entry into the elite group of nations capable of developing fifth-generation aircraft.

‘Manthan’ & iDEX Pavilion

Focusing on start-ups and innovation in aerospace and defence, this pavilion will showcase indigenous solutions under the ‘Make in India’ and ‘Aatmanirbhar Bharat’ initiatives.

Strategic Importance of Aero India 2025

Strengthening India’s Defence & Aerospace Industry

The exhibition encourages collaboration between domestic and foreign firms, boosting indigenous defence manufacturing under initiatives like ‘Make in India’ and ‘Aatmanirbhar Bharat’.

Diplomatic & Economic Significance

The event enhances India’s position as a global aerospace hub and strengthens defence ties with major global players.

Innovation & Self-Reliance

Aero India promotes domestic R&D and high-tech manufacturing capabilities, bridging technological gaps through strategic partnerships.

Conclusion

Aero India 2025 stands as a testament to India’s growing prowess in aerospace and defence. By fostering innovation, strengthening international collaborations, and emphasizing self-reliance, the event not only showcases India’s capabilities but also sets the stage for future advancements in the sector.
