One Nation, One Subscription (ONOS) Scheme

Overview

The ONOS scheme aims to expand access to top research journals for State-affiliated colleges and students across India, starting January 1, 2025.

Key Features

ONOS will increase the number of accessible journals by 62%, covering 95% of published research, benefiting approximately 1.8 crore students, faculty, and researchers.

Financial and Implementation Details

The Centre’s annual spend on subscriptions will rise from ₹1,000-1,500 crore to ₹6,000 crore (2025-2027). The scheme will be coordinated through INFLIBNET.

Article Processing Charges (APC) Fund

A ₹150 crore fund will support researchers in making their work freely accessible by paying Article Processing Charges (APCs).

Accessibility and Broad Impact

The scheme allows students and faculty to access research papers remotely, helping bridge the knowledge gap and democratize access to high-quality research across India.
