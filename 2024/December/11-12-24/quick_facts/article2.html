India Skills Report Ranks Kerala Among Top States for Employability

Relevance: GS 3 (Employment)

Kerala's Ranking

Kerala ranks 5th in employability in India with a rate of 71%, following Maharashtra, Delhi, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh. It is among the top states for employment opportunities.

Youth Employability

Kerala has a high employability rate of 87.47% for youths aged 22-25, ranking it among the top states. For experienced professionals (26-29 years), Kerala leads with 68.82% employability.

Female Employability

Kerala stands third in female employability, making it a preferred state for female job seekers.

Overall Employability in India

Nationwide, 54.81% of youths have the skills required for employment. The study surveyed 6.50 lakh youths through the Global Employability Test conducted by Wheebox.

Contributors to Kerala's Success

Kerala's success is driven by high literacy rates, a robust educational system, and a skilled workforce that contributes to its strong talent pool.
