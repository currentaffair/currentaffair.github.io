Ayush Visa and e-Ayush Visa Overview

Why in News? As of December 4, 2024, India has issued 123 regular Ayush visas, 221 e-Ayush visas, and 17 e-Ayush attendant visas to promote the Ayush system of medicine internationally.

Key Highlights

The Ayush Visa is for foreign visitors seeking treatment under India's Ayush system. There are four main categories:

  • Ayush Visa: For those seeking therapeutic care and wellness treatment in accredited hospitals or wellness centers.
  • Ayush Attendant Visa: For attendants accompanying a foreigner on an Ayush Visa.
  • e-Ayush Visa: An online visa option for those seeking Ayush treatment.
  • e-Ayush Attendant Visa: An online visa for attendants accompanying e-Ayush Visa holders.

These initiatives are aimed at promoting the Ayush system of medicine and attracting international patients to India.
