India-Japan Forum 2024

Overview

At the India-Japan Forum 2024, India's External Affairs Minister emphasized a strategic semiconductor alliance between India and Japan to strengthen global supply chain resilience. Both nations are collaborating with Taiwan to reduce dependence on China and support India's semiconductor mission.

Semiconductor Collaboration

India and Japan are working together to enhance semiconductor industries, aiming to mitigate supply chain risks. This partnership supports both countries' technological autonomy and aligns with broader Indo-Pacific strategies.

Quad's Growth

The Quad (India, Japan, Australia, US) was revitalized in 2017 under the Trump administration. It focuses on regional security, economic cooperation, and countering China’s influence through inclusive burden-sharing and regular summits.

India-Japan Forum

The India-Japan Forum serves as a platform for collaboration and strategic partnership. It fosters dialogue, mutual trust, and future cooperation, following Chatham House rules for confidentiality.

Quadrilateral Security Dialogue (Quad)

The Quad promotes security and economic growth in the Indo-Pacific, countering China’s assertiveness. Established in 2007, it gained momentum after 2017, focusing on joint military drills, economic initiatives, and regional security.
