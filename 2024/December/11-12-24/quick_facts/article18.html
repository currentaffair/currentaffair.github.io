Ayush Visa and e-Ayush Visa

Key Points

As of December 4, 2024, a total of 123 regular Ayush visas, 221 e-Ayush visas, and 17 e-Ayush attendant visas have been issued.

Ayush Visa Categories

  • Ayush Visa: For foreign visitors seeking treatment under the Ayush system in accredited hospitals or wellness centers.
  • Ayush Attendant Visa: For attendants accompanying a foreigner on an Ayush Visa.
  • e-Ayush Visa: An online visa option for those seeking Ayush treatment.
  • e-Ayush Attendant Visa: An online visa for attendants accompanying patients with e-Ayush Visas.

Objective

The Ayush Visa system aims to promote the Ayush system of medicine internationally, making it easier for foreign patients to seek treatment in India.
