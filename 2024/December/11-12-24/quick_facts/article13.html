India Skills Report: Kerala's Employability Ranking

Kerala's Employability Rate

Kerala ranks fifth in India with an employability rate of 71%, following Maharashtra, Delhi, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh.

Youth Employability

For the 22-25 age group, Kerala’s employability rate is 87.47%, positioning it among the top states. For professionals aged 26-29, Kerala has the highest employability rate at 68.82%.

Female Employability

Kerala is highly preferred by female job seekers, ranking third for female employability opportunities.

Overall Employability in India

Nationally, 54.81% of surveyed youth possess the necessary skills for employment. The study surveyed 6.50 lakh youths via the Global Employability Test conducted by Wheebox and its partners.

Contributors to Kerala’s Success

Kerala’s success is attributed to its high literacy rates, strong educational infrastructure, and a skilled youth workforce that contributes to its robust talent pool.
