India's Internet Shutdowns

Supreme Court Ruling

The Supreme Court has recognized the Internet as a fundamental right. It ruled that any restriction on Internet access must be lawful, temporary, necessary, and proportionate, subject to judicial review.

Impact and Call for Reform

Internet shutdowns in India cause significant economic losses and disrupt daily life. The 2020 amendments to Telecom Suspension Rules have made enforcement more difficult. Calls for reform emphasize greater compliance with Supreme Court guidelines and statutory recognition.
