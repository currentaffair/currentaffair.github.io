Myanmar Border Security & Development Initiatives

Overview

The Union Home Ministry focuses on strengthening Myanmar border security, phasing out the Free Movement Regime (FMR), and promoting peace and development in the Northeast region of India.

Key Details

  • Demographic Mapping: Mapping in Nagaland, Mizoram, and Manipur to support border fencing and curb infiltration.
  • FMR Scrapping: Plans to regulate movement within a reduced 10 km limit along the Myanmar border.
  • Unfenced Border: 1,643 km of unfenced border with Myanmar, across multiple Indian states.
  • Development Mapping: Mapping of mineral, oil, and coal reserves to increase revenue.
  • Left-Wing Extremism (LWE): Security forces eliminated 287 Naxalites and aim to eradicate LWE by March 31, 2026.
  • Drug Trafficking Control: Major drug seizures and destruction of over 117,000 kg of narcotics in 2024.
