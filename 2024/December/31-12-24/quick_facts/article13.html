Increased Regulatory Action on Nidhi Companies

Key Highlights

In 2024, the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) and Registrars of Companies (RoCs) intensified action on Nidhi companies to ensure transparency, curb illicit activities, and strengthen corporate governance.

Regulatory Actions

  • 131 orders issued against Nidhis, a 72% rise from 2023.
  • Penalties ranging from Rs 10,000 to Rs 30 lakh for non-compliance.

Beneficial Ownership

Beneficial Ownership refers to individuals who ultimately own or control a company, even if shares are held by another. Companies must disclose those with 25% or more ownership.

Nidhi Companies

  • Non-banking financial entities (NBFCs) operating under Section 406 of the Companies Act, 2013.
  • Encourage savings and provide loans exclusively to members.
  • Do not require an RBI license but must follow strict disclosure norms.
  • Registered as public limited companies with "Nidhi Limited" in their name.
  • Must maintain at least 200 members and Rs 20 lakh in net owned funds.
