ADB Loans for India's Green Growth and Logistics Modernization

Why in News?

The Indian Government and ADB signed two loans to strengthen infrastructure and support sustainable growth in India.

Key Facts

The USD 500 million loan focuses on green infrastructure projects, supporting sectors like urban development, energy transition, and healthcare. The USD 350 million loan will bolster the manufacturing and logistics sectors, aiming to reduce logistics costs and promote innovation.

SMILE Program

The SMILE program, backed by ADB, aims to modernize India's logistics sector. It focuses on improving logistics efficiency through better coordination, infrastructure, technology, and trade processes.

Government Initiatives

  • Bharatmala Pariyojana
  • PM Gati Shakti Scheme
  • Multi Modal Logistics Parks
  • National Logistics Policy 2022

About ADB

ADB is a multilateral bank established to foster economic growth in the Asia-Pacific. India is a founding member and key shareholder.
