Underutilization of Operation Greens Scheme

The Parliamentary Standing Committee (PSC) report highlights the underperformance of the Operation Greens (OG) scheme, focusing on its limited success and concerns regarding agricultural market volatility.

Key Findings of the PSC Report

Underutilization of Budget: Only 34% of the Rs 173.40 crore allocated for 2024-25 has been spent by October 2024.
Limited Impact on Price Stabilization: Onion prices in Maharashtra have crashed by nearly 50%, and potato shortages are observed in Odisha and Jharkhand.
Policy Inconsistencies: Government policies like export bans and duties have frustrated farmers.

What is Operation Greens Scheme?

Launched in 2018, Operation Greens aims to stabilize the prices of perishable crops and reduce post-harvest losses. Initially focused on Tomato, Onion, and Potato (TOP), its scope expanded to 22 perishable crops including fruits and vegetables.
