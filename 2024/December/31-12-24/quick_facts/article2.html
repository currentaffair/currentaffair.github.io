Kaveri Engine Development

Introduction

The Kaveri engine, approved for in-flight testing, marks a crucial step in India's aerospace development. It's designed for the Light Combat Aircraft (LCA) Tejas and UAVs like Ghatak.

Ghatak UCAV

The Ghatak is an autonomous stealth UCAV developed for the Indian Air Force, powered by a turbofan engine, with its first flight planned for 2025.

Challenges

Delays arose due to engine complexity, but global collaborations helped address these technological gaps.

Significance

The Kaveri engine ensures strategic autonomy, reduces dependence on foreign manufacturers, and boosts India's aerospace industry.

Way Forward

The next steps include ensuring reliable testing, expanding partnerships, and preparing for mass production.
