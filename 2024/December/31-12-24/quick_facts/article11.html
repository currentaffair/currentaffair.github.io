Prime Minister’s Internship Scheme

Overview

The Prime Minister’s Internship Scheme aims to provide internship opportunities to 1 crore youth in top 500 companies over five years. The goal is to bridge the gap between education and employment and expose youth to real-world business environments.

Scheme Details

  • Applications Received: 6.21 lakh for 1.27 lakh opportunities.
  • Internship Duration: 12 months.
  • Financial Assistance: ₹5,000/month (₹4,500 from the government, ₹500 from companies).
  • One-Time Grant: ₹6,000 for incidentals upon joining.
  • Selection Process: Ongoing.

Target & Implementation

The scheme is targeting youth across India, focusing on skill-building and enhancing employability. It aims to foster entrepreneurship and exposure to various professions.
