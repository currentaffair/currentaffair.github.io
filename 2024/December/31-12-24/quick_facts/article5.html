SpaDeX Autonomous Space Docking Mission

Mission Overview

The SpaDeX mission aims to master autonomous space docking technology, positioning India among the countries with space docking expertise (USA, Russia, China).

Key Objectives

  • Mission Components: Two satellites, SDX01 (Chaser) and SDX02 (Target), launched into a 470 km low-Earth orbit via PSLV.
  • Rendezvous and Docking: Testing docking and undocking technology for future missions.
  • Lunar Mission Relevance: Critical for missions like Chandrayaan-4.

Space Exploration & Advancements

  • Payloads: 24 payloads, advancing space exploration and ISRO's capabilities.
  • Support for Astronauts: Key technology for astronaut missions, sample returns, and space infrastructure.
  • Boost for Startups: 10 experiments from Indian space startups and private universities.
  • Agriculture in Space: Studies on seed germination and plant growth (CROPS module) focusing on crops like spinach and cowpeas.
