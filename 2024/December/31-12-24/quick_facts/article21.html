Conjugal Visits in Prisons

The Delhi Government is reconsidering a proposal to allow conjugal visits for prisoners.

What are Conjugal Visits?

Conjugal visits allow inmates to spend private time, including intimate moments, with their legal spouses within the prison, promoting rehabilitation and psychological well-being.

Judicial Landmarks

Important judicial decisions include:
- 2014: Punjab & Haryana High Court upheld the right to procreation for prisoners.
- 2018: Madras High Court allowed parole for conjugal relations.
- 2023: Tamil Nadu urged to consider conjugal visits within prison precincts.

Global Practices

Conjugal visits are allowed in some US states (not federal prisons). In Europe, countries like Spain, France, Sweden, and Denmark permit such visits, with Spain offering monthly and Sweden up to 9-hour sessions.
