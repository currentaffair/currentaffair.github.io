Prime Minister’s Internship Scheme

Overview

The Prime Minister’s Internship Scheme aims to provide internships to one crore youth over five years in the top 500 companies, exposing them to real-world business environments and creating employment opportunities.

Key Details

  • Applications Received: 6.21 lakh for 1.27 lakh opportunities
  • Internship Duration: 12 months
  • Financial Assistance: ₹5,000 per month (₹4,500 from Union Govt, ₹500 from the company)
  • One-Time Grant: ₹6,000 for incidentals upon joining

Target and Goals

The scheme targets youth across India, aiming to bridge the gap between education and employment, enhance employability, and foster entrepreneurship.

Implementation

Launched by the Union Corporate Affairs Ministry, the scheme provides skill-building opportunities to youth, contributing to both personal growth and industry development.
