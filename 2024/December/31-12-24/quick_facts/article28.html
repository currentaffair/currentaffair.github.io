Kashmir's Papier Mache Revives the Dodo

Kashmir's papier mache artisans are crafting and exporting dodo figurines, particularly to Mauritius and Europe, around Christmas 2024.

Dodo

The dodo (Raphus cucullatus) is an extinct bird from Mauritius, becoming extinct in 1681 due to human activity and invasive species.

Papier Mache Art

Papier mache is a 600-year-old craft in Kashmir, involving colorful paper pulp objects, influenced by Persian motifs and recently incorporating dodo designs.

Other Crafts of Kashmir

Seven Kashmiri crafts, including pashmina, sozni, and kashmiri-carpets, have received Geographical Indication (GI) tags.
