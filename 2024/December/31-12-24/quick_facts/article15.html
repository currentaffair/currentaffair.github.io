ADB Loans for India's Green Growth and Logistics Modernization

Why in News?

India and the Asian Development Bank (ADB) have signed two significant loans aimed at supporting sustainable infrastructure and logistics modernization in India.

Key Facts on the Loans

Two loans: USD 500 million for green infrastructure and USD 350 million for logistics sector improvements. These loans focus on climate commitments, connectivity, energy transition, and under-resourced sectors like urban development, education, and healthcare.

SMILE Program

The SMILE program focuses on improving India's manufacturing competitiveness and supply chain resilience by enhancing logistics efficiency. It promotes infrastructure development, standardization, and technology adoption.

Government Initiatives for Logistics

  • Bharatmala Pariyojana
  • PM Gati Shakti Scheme
  • Multi Modal Logistics Parks
  • National Logistics Policy 2022

About ADB

The Asian Development Bank (ADB) fosters economic growth in the Asia-Pacific region. India is a founding member and plays a significant role in ADB's support for sustainable growth initiatives.
