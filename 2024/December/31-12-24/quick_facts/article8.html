Myanmar Border Security Initiatives

Overview

The Union Home Ministry has prioritized strengthening border security, phasing out the Free Movement Regime (FMR) with Myanmar, and fostering peace and development in the Northeast region.

Key Details

  • Demographic Mapping: Mapping data in Nagaland, Mizoram, and Manipur to support border fencing and curb infiltration.
  • Free Movement Regime (FMR): FMR will be scrapped, restricting movement within a 10 km limit.
  • Unfenced Border: Myanmar shares a 1,643 km largely unfenced border with India.

Development & Security Measures

  • Mineral Mapping: Mapping of mineral, oil, and coal reserves to boost revenue.
  • Peace & Prosperity: Efforts to promote regional stability and unity.
  • Security Achievements: 287 Naxalites eliminated, 992 arrested. Target to eradicate Left-Wing Extremism (LWE) by 2026.

Drug Trafficking Control

Over 1,17,284 kg of narcotics destroyed in 2024, signaling strong action against drug cartels.
