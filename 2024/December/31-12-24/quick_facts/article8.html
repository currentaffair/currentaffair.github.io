Militant Activity Rise in Jammu Region (2024)

Overview

The Jammu region has experienced a significant increase in militant activity in 2024, with rising casualties among security forces and evolving militant tactics.

Casualty Statistics

  • Jammu Region: 18 security personnel killed vs 13 militants in around 30 encounters.
  • Kashmir Region: 55 militants killed, with 10 security personnel casualties.
  • Higher ratio of security personnel killed in Jammu region, highlighting new militant strategies.

Militant Tactics & Key Areas

  • Militants used challenging terrain, such as forests and ridges, to avoid detection.
  • The Pir Panjal Valley (Rajouri & Poonch) has seen a resurgence of militancy since 2020.
  • Militants targeted civilians, with 14 civilian deaths in Jammu, including 9 pilgrims.

Security Measures & Crackdown

  • NSG's Black Cats unit deployed in Jammu due to rising militant threats.
  • Security forces are on high alert, with ongoing crackdowns on militants and their supporters.
  • 168 locals arrested for supporting militants under the Unlawful Activities (Prevention) Act.

Key Attacks

  • Ganderbal Attack (October 2024): Militants targeted a strategic tunnel project.
  • Reasi Attack (June 2024): Attack on pilgrims leading to civilian casualties.

Long-Term Implications

The resurgence of militancy in Jammu signals a shift towards a fully-fledged insurgency, with evolving tactics and the need for enhanced counter-insurgency strategies.
