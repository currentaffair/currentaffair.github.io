Universal Immunization Programme and Mumps Vaccine

Context

Tamil Nadu has proposed including the mumps vaccine in the Universal Immunization Programme (UIP) due to a rise in mumps cases. Currently, mumps is not covered under UIP, which includes the Measles-Rubella (MR) vaccine introduced in 2017.

Universal Immunization Programme (UIP)

UIP is a major public health initiative by the Government of India aimed at providing free vaccines for preventable diseases to children and pregnant women, reducing mortality and morbidity.

Mission Indradhanush

Launched in 2014, Mission Indradhanush aims to improve immunization coverage in underserved areas. Over 54.6 million children and 13.2 million pregnant women have been vaccinated as of December 2024.
