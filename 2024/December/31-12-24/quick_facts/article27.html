Underutilization of Operation Greens Scheme

Operation Greens scheme faces underperformance according to a recent Parliamentary Standing Committee (PSC) report.

Key Findings

  • Underutilization of Budget: Only 34% of the allocated Rs 173.40 crore has been spent by October 2024.
  • Limited Impact: Onion prices in Maharashtra have dropped by 50%, while potato shortages persist in Odisha and Jharkhand.
  • Policy Issues: Inconsistent government policies, like export bans, hinder price stabilization.

About the Scheme

Operation Greens aims to stabilize the prices of perishable crops and improve farmers' earnings through support for clusters, infrastructure, and value addition.

Initially focusing on Tomato, Onion, and Potato (TOP), the scheme now covers 22 crops, including fruits and vegetables.
