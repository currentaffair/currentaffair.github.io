Emission Reduction & Opinion Trading

Emission Reduction Targets

The United States aims for 50-52% reduction in greenhouse gas emissions by 2030, with a further goal of 61-66% by 2035. India targets reducing the GHG intensity of GDP by 33-45% by 2030, with major contributions from renewable energy and carbon sequestration.

Renewable Energy & Technology

The U.S. targets 100% carbon pollution-free electricity by 2035. India plans to expand solar, wind, and hydrogen energy technologies, and aims for a net-zero economy by 2070.

Opinion Trading in India

Opinion trading platforms in India allow users to bet on future events. Despite rapid growth and significant investment, legal challenges persist, as these platforms operate in a regulatory grey area. A comprehensive regulatory framework is needed for transparency and user protection.
