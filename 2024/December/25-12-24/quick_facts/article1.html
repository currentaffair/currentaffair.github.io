AIMC: Accelerating National Highway Projects

Context

The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH) is implementing Automated & Intelligent Machine-aided Construction (AIMC) to address delays in national highway projects.

What is the AIMC System?

AIMC combines smart machines and real-time data sharing. It is being tested on the Lucknow-Kanpur Motorway (Awadh Motorway) and will help monitor construction progress, ensuring quality and efficiency.

Why is AIMC Needed?

AIMC addresses delays caused by outdated technology, inaccurate information, and contractor performance. It will improve efficiency, increase road durability, and ensure timely project completion.

How Do AIMC Machines Work?

  • GPS-Aided Motor Grader: Uses GNSS data to position the grader's blade with precision.
  • Intelligent Compaction Roller (IC Roller): Ensures material stability by reducing air pockets in soil or concrete.
  • Vibratory Rollers: Compact soil and base layers to ensure stability in the road structure.

Way Forward

AIMC will reduce delays and improve construction by automating processes, providing real-time data, and enabling nighttime construction. This will ensure timely project completion.
