Preserving Jahaz Haveli - Diwan Todar Mal

Why in the News?

The Punjab government is set to preserve and restore Jahaz Haveli, once the residence of Diwan Todar Mal, a significant historical figure from the Mughal era.

About Diwan Todar Mal

Diwan Todar Mal was a wealthy merchant and a prominent revenue official during the Mughal reign of Emperor Akbar and Shah Jahan. He was also a member of the Navaratnas, Akbar's group of nine extraordinary people.

Career Background

Starting as a writer, Todar Mal rose through the ranks under Sher Shah Suri and the Mughal emperors. He was in charge of building the fort of Rohtas and later continued his service under Akbar.
