INS Nirdeshak

Introduction

The Indian Navy has commissioned INS Nirdeshak, a state-of-the-art survey vessel, at Visakhapatnam Naval Dockyard.

About INS Nirdeshak

INS Nirdeshak, the second ship of the Survey Vessels (Large) (SVL) Project, is designed to enhance hydrographic surveys, navigation, and maritime safety. It uses advanced systems like Multi-Beam Echo Sounders and Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs).

SVL Project and Indigenous Manufacturing

The SVL Project modernizes the Indian Navy’s hydrographic survey fleet by replacing the aging Sandhayak class ships. Built with over 80% indigenous content, the project supports India’s Make in India and Atma Nirbhar Bharat initiatives.

Maritime Diplomacy and Security

The ship will support India’s SAGAR initiative and enhance maritime security in the Indian Ocean Region. It will also assist friendly nations in oceanic surveys, furthering India’s leadership in the region.
