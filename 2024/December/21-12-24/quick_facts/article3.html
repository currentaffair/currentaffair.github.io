Constitution and Individual Freedom

Community Identity vs. Individual Freedom

Community identity based on caste, religion, or nation can limit individual freedom, reducing unique identities and autonomy.

Impact of Executive Power

The expansion of executive power undermines constitutional checks and balances, eroding democratic principles.

Economic Justice and Social Equality

The Constitution recognizes the need for continuous democratic debate on economic justice and inclusion, essential for democracy's success.

Modern Political Challenges

Modern politics conflicts with the Constitution's goal of freeing individuals from fixed identities, pushing for permanent divisions based on caste or religion.

Constitutional Provisions for Dignity

The Indian Constitution includes Fundamental Rights and Directive Principles to safeguard individual dignity, including rights to equality, freedom, and protection from exploitation.
