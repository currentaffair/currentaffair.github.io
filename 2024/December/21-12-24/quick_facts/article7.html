Sacred Groves in India

About Sacred Groves

Sacred groves are small areas of trees protected by local communities due to religious and cultural significance, also crucial for biodiversity conservation.

Types of Sacred Groves

1. Traditional Sacred Groves
2. Temple Groves
3. Groves around Burial Grounds

Importance of Sacred Groves

Sacred groves play a vital role in biodiversity conservation, aquifer recharge, soil conservation, and serve as refuges for rare species.

Threats

Urbanization, encroachment, and the loss of traditional beliefs threaten sacred groves. Invasive species also pose a danger to their integrity.

Conservation Strategies

Key strategies include nationwide surveys, marking boundaries, empowering local communities, and official recognition under national policies like the Wildlife Protection Act.

Success Story: Piplantri Village

Piplantri village turned barren land into thriving groves by planting 111 trees for every girl child, providing sustainable livelihoods and improving community well-being.
