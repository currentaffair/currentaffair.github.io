Human Error Led to 19 of 34 IAF Crashes in 2017-2022

Overview

Between 2017 and 2022, the Indian Air Force (IAF) experienced 34 air accidents, with human error accounting for 19 of these crashes.

Key Crashes

  • Mi-17V5 Helicopter Crash: On December 8, 2021, General Bipin Rawat was killed due to unexpected weather changes leading to pilot disorientation and a controlled flight into terrain (CFIT).
  • MIG-21 Fighter Jet & Mi-17 Helicopter: On February 27, 2019, both aircraft were lost during an aerial dogfight with the Pakistan Air Force post-Balakot air strike.
