Vice-President Jagdeep Dhankhar's Remarks on Parliamentary Accountability

Vice-President and Rajya Sabha Chairman Jagdeep Dhankhar emphasized the importance of accountability in Parliament, pointing out the decline in parliamentary productivity during the Winter Session. He urged lawmakers to be responsible, as citizens are demanding answers about their roles.

Chaudhary Charan Singh: A Tribute to India's Agriculture Leader

National Farmers' Day (Kisan Diwas) 2024 honored the birth anniversary of Chaudhary Charan Singh, a key figure in India's agricultural and rural development. His legacy includes contributions to the Debt Redemption Bill, Minimum Support Price (MSP), and rural reforms.

Key Contributions of Chaudhary Charan Singh

  • Advocated for agricultural reforms, including the Land Holding Act (1960) and Consolidation of Holdings Act (1953).
  • Promoted decentralization, local economic autonomy, and grassroots governance.
  • Honored with the Bharat Ratna in 2024 for his pioneering work in rural development.
