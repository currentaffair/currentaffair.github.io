Andhra Pradesh Skill Census Launch

Andhra Pradesh will launch Indiaâ€™s first statewide skill census in January 2025, aiming to identify skill gaps and strengths in its workforce. This initiative builds on a successful pilot in Mangalagiri, covering over 1 lakh households and nearly 3 lakh residents.

Key Objectives

  • Map existing workforce skills and identify gaps.
  • Reduce youth unemployment, particularly in the 15-29 age group.
  • Align skills with industry demands to boost employability.
  • Enhance economic growth through targeted interventions.

Implementation & Challenges

The census will cover 3.5 crore working-age individuals across 1.8 crore households. Challenges include concerns about privacy, the workload of surveyors, and a high dropout rate among youth. However, the census aims to boost labor participation, especially for women and youth.

Potential Outcomes

  • Better policy-making and vocational training programs.
  • Increased job creation and workforce readiness.
  • Enhanced data for targeted skilling programs.
