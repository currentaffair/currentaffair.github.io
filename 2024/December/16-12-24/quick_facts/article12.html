Human Evolution and Migration

Why in News?

Scientists have established that Homo sapiens evolved in Africa and later migrated worldwide. The routes and timing of these migrations remain debated.

Migration Theories

Coastal Dispersion Theory

This theory suggests early humans migrated along coastlines, benefiting from warm climates and abundant food. Evidence points to coastal migration to Australia via the Indian Ocean around 65,000 years ago.

Conclusion

Theories about human migration continue to evolve. Evidence challenges coastal migration and supports broader dispersal patterns, integrating genetic and archaeological findings.
