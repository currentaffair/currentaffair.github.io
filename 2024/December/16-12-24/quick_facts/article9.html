Yuva Sahakar Scheme

The Minister of Cooperation informed the Lok Sabha about the Yuva Sahakar Scheme, an initiative by the National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) to support newly formed cooperative societies with innovative ideas. The scheme encourages young entrepreneurs by offering long-term loans for project activities.

Key Features

  • Linked to a Rs. 1000 crore Cooperative Start-up and Innovation Fund.
  • Provides long-term loans up to 5 years.
  • Offers a 2% interest subvention on applicable loan rates.
  • Additional incentives for cooperatives in the North-Eastern region.
  • Exclusive benefits for women and Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe candidates.

About NCDC

The National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) was established under the NCDC Act of 1962. It aims to promote cooperative development in sectors such as agriculture, industrial goods, and livestock, offering financial assistance and development support.
