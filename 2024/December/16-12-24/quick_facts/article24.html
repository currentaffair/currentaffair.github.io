Jalvahak Scheme for Cargo Movement

Objective

The Jalvahak scheme promotes eco-friendly, economical, and efficient cargo transport to decongest railways and roadways by using National Waterways 1, 2, and 16.

Incentives

Up to 35% reimbursement of operating costs for vessels, with a focus on vessels owned or operated by government entities.

Operational Details

  • Fixed-day scheduled sailing services launched from Kolkata to Patna, Varanasi (NW-1), and Guwahati (NW-2) via the Indo-Bangladesh Protocol Route.
  • UltraTech Cement was the first company to use NW-1 for large-scale gypsum transport.

Significance

The scheme optimizes supply chains, strengthens the logistics network, and benefits major shipping companies, boosting the role of waterways in Indiaâ€™s logistics network.
