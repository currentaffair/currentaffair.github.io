How Would a Carbon Market Function?

COP29 in Baku approved standards to establish an international carbon market by next year, aiming to curb global carbon emissions.

Challenges & Concerns

  • Developing nations: Face barriers in carbon accounting.
  • Corporates: Prefer voluntary reporting over strict regulations.
  • Governments: Could manipulate the credit supply.
  • Offsets: Misuse of offsets may undermine genuine emission cuts.
