Over 78% of Top Paid H-1B Applicants Were From India

Recent data reveals that Indian professionals dominate the high-paid H-1B visa segment, with a significant role played by smaller U.S. companies.

Key Points

  • Indian Dominance: 78% of high-paid H-1B applicants (earning over $1M annually) are from India, with over 25% being women.
  • Company Sponsorship: 65% of these high-paid Indian applicants are sponsored by smaller U.S. firms, which offer higher salaries than Indian-based companies.
  • H-1B Process: Employers submit petitions in an annual lottery (April), detailing salary and job information.
  • Data Summary: Out of ~100,000 approved petitions (2021-2024), 123 had salaries above $1M; 96 were from India, mostly from smaller companies.
