Yuva Sahakar Scheme

Overview

The Yuva Sahakar Scheme is an initiative by the National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) aimed at supporting newly formed cooperative societies with innovative ideas. It encourages young entrepreneurs by providing long-term loans for project activities.

Key Features

  • Fund of Rs. 1000 crores under the Cooperative Start-up and Innovation Fund.
  • Long-term loans up to 5 years with a 2% interest subvention.
  • Additional incentives for cooperatives in the North-Eastern region and for women, Scheduled Caste, and Scheduled Tribe candidates.

About NCDC

The NCDC is a statutory corporation established in 1962 to promote cooperative development in various sectors. It provides financial assistance and development support for agricultural produce, foodstuffs, industrial goods, and other commodities.
