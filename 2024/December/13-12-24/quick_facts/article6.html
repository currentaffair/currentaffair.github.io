Lok Sabha Passes Bill to Amend the Disaster Management Act of 2005

Introduction

The Lok Sabha passed the Disaster Management (Amendment) Bill, 2024, aimed at enhancing disaster management mechanisms in India. The Bill seeks to improve the efficiency of national and state disaster management authorities.

Purpose of the Bill

The Bill is designed to strengthen disaster response frameworks and address the implementation challenges raised by state governments under the 2005 Act.

Criticism from the Opposition

The opposition raised concerns that the Bill adds more organizations rather than streamlining the disaster management process. They also pointed out the Bill's failure to adequately address climate change and compensation policies.

Amendments Rejected

Several proposed amendments by the Opposition were rejected during the legislative process.
