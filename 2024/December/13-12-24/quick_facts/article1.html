No Power to Monitor Quota Execution

Overview

The Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT) clarified it cannot supervise other ministries on implementing reservation policies for SCs, STs, and OBCs.

Key Points

  • DoPT lacks authority over other Ministries for quota execution.
  • DoPT cannot conduct surprise checks on reservation rosters due to the Transaction of Business Rules.
  • Liaison officers are responsible for monitoring the implementation across government departments.
  • The Parliamentary Standing Committee expressed concerns about the effectiveness of the system.
