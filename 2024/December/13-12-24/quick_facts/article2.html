Growth Slowdown Analysis

Overview

India's GDP growth dropped to 5.4% in Q2 FY 2024-25, marking the lowest growth in the past 7 quarters.

Possible Causes

  • Seasonal factors like rains and festivals
  • Post-COVID adjustments in private sector spending

Projection

India is projected to achieve 6.5%-7% growth for FY 2024-25, according to the Economic Survey.
