Gukesh: Youngest World Chess Champion

Historic Victory

D. Gukesh, an 18-year-old from Chennai, made history by defeating defending champion Ding Liren after 58 moves to secure the required 7.5 points and win the World Chess Championship. A critical error on Ding's 55th move allowed Gukesh to claim victory.

Milestones Achieved

Gukesh is now the youngest-ever World Chess Champion. This match marked the first World Championship final contested between two Asian players.

Asian Chess Legacy

Gukesh becomes only the third Asian to win the title, following Viswanathan Anand (his mentor) and Ding Liren.
