Heritage Conservation and Key Sites in India

INTACH

The Indian National Trust for Art and Cultural Heritage (INTACH) works for heritage conservation across India. It has over 228 chapters and is responsible for restoring monuments, promoting heritage awareness, and establishing educational programs.

Lonar Lake

Lonar Lake, in Maharashtra, is a unique meteorite-formed lake with saline and alkaline water. It is recognized for its ecological importance and historical significance, with temples dating back over 1250 years. Maharashtra is planning to propose it for UNESCO World Heritage status.

Other Notable Lakes in India

India is home to several significant lakes, such as Vembanad, Chilika, and Dal Lake. These lakes support biodiversity, tourism, and environmental sustainability, with some being Ramsar sites or wildlife sanctuaries.
