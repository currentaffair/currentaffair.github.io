CEA’s Address on Mental Health and Economic Priorities

Overview

The Chief Economic Advisor (CEA) has expressed concern over the growing mental health crisis among the youth in India, highlighting its implications for economic priorities.

Concerns Raised

The CEA pointed out the rising anxiety, depression, and suicidal tendencies, attributed to factors such as:

  • Excessive screen time
  • Sedentary lifestyle
  • Ultra-processed food consumption

Importance

The importance of addressing mental health is vital for leveraging India’s demographic dividend, as mental health complements physical health for productive employment.

Call for Action

The responsibility to address mental health issues extends beyond the government to include society and the private sector.
