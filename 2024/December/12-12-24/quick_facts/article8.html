Anti-Obesity Benefits of Fermented Bamboo Shoot

Context: A study by Indian researchers has found that traditional fermented bamboo shoot from Tripura has anti-obesity properties.

About 'Melye-Amiley'

'Melye-amiley' is a traditional fermented bamboo shoot from Tripura, known for its anti-obesity effects. It helps in weight management by reducing lipid accumulation and enhancing fatty acid Î²-oxidation.

Research Findings

In vitro cell culture studies show that this fermented bamboo shoot variety reduces intracellular lipid accumulation. It increases the expression of lipolytic and fat browning genes, contributing to weight loss and metabolic health.

Significance

The study suggests that the fermented bamboo shoot extract could serve as a natural, cost-effective alternative to conventional weight-loss methods by boosting energy expenditure in fat cells.

About Bamboo

Bamboos are diverse, evergreen perennial plants from the grass family Poaceae. They are known for their great size, with giant bamboos being the largest members. The word "bamboo" is thought to originate from Malay or Kannada through Dutch or Portuguese.
