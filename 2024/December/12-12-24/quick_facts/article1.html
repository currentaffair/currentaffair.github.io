MuleHunter.AI and Mule Bank Accounts

Context: The Reserve Bank of India developed MuleHunter.AI to tackle "mule" bank accounts, often used for illegal activities like money laundering.

What is a Mule Bank Account?

A mule bank account is a legitimate account used by criminals for illegal financial activities. Often, the account holder is unaware that their account is being exploited for money laundering.

What is a Money Mule?

A money mule is an individual whose account is used to launder illicit funds, typically unknowingly. When discovered, the money mule becomes the focus of investigations while the real criminals remain undetected.

Problem of Mule Bank Accounts

In India, mule accounts are a significant issue. Around 4.5 lakh mule accounts have been frozen due to cybercrime proceeds, with large banks like SBI, PNB, and Kotak Mahindra Bank reporting high numbers of these accounts.

Role of MuleHunter.AI

MuleHunter.AI, developed by the RBI Innovation Hub, helps banks identify mule accounts using artificial intelligence. The model has shown promising results in pilot tests with public sector banks.

Government Actions Against Mule Accounts

The government, in collaboration with the RBI, I4C, NABARD, and other stakeholders, is addressing the issue by encouraging banks to use AI and machine learning for real-time detection of mule accounts and improving fraud prevention practices.
