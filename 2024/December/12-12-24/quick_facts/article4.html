Jasleen Kaur Wins Turner Prize 2024

Context: Jasleen Kaur, an Indian-origin artist, has won the prestigious Turner Prize in 2024.

About the Turner Prize

The Turner Prize, named after English painter J.M.W. Turner (1775-1851), was first awarded in 1984. It aims to encourage interest in contemporary art and is one of the most prestigious art awards globally.

Prize Details

  • Winner receives £25,000; shortlisted artists receive £10,000.
  • Associated with conceptual art.
  • Eligibility: British artists or immigrants working in Britain.

Notable Past Winners

  • Malcolm Morley (1984)
  • Anish Kapoor (1991, first Indian-origin winner)
  • Wolfgang Tillmans (2000, first photographer and non-British winner)

Jasleen Kaur's Winning Exhibition

Her exhibition honored her family's migration history to Scotland, contributing to her win.
