Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS)

TISS is set to appoint a permanent vice-chancellor after a year-long delay. Established in 1936, it was recognized as a university by the Government of India in 1964.

Deemed University Status

TISS is a deemed university, granted this status by the Government under the University Grants Commission (UGC) Act of 1956. It holds the same academic privileges as universities.

Expansion and Campuses

TISS has expanded its campuses over the years, including locations in Mumbai, Tuljapur, Guwahati, Hyderabad, and others across India.

Academic Programs

The institute offers a range of postgraduate and doctoral programs in fields like Social Work, Law, Gender Studies, Disaster Studies, and more.

Global Collaborations

TISS collaborates with prestigious institutions such as the University of Chicago and the London School of Economics.

Social Work and Field Action

TISS is known for its fieldwork, participating in disaster response efforts such as during India's partition and the Bhopal disaster.

Governance Structure

The institute is governed by a board appointed by the Government of India, Maharashtra, and UGC, with academic matters handled by a council of experts.
