Malwa Canal and Kaleshwaram Lift Irrigation Scheme

Malwa Canal Project

The Malwa Canal is a proposed irrigation and water management project in Punjab, India. It will run parallel to the Rajasthan Feeder Canal and aims to irrigate 2,000,000 hectares of land in southern Punjab. The canal will be constructed from the Harike structure in Ferozepur and terminate near the Haryana border.

Kaleshwaram Lift Irrigation Scheme (KLIS)

The Kaleshwaram Lift Irrigation Scheme (KLIS) is a large irrigation project in Telangana, India. It draws water from the Godavari River and serves over 45,000 acres. KLIS provides water for irrigation, municipal, and industrial use, benefiting multiple districts in the state.
