India's Net Direct Tax Collection Growth

Overview of Growth

India's net direct tax collections have increased by 16.5% to ₹15.82 lakh crore as of December 17, 2024. Non-corporate tax receipts saw a stronger rise, growing by 22.5% to ₹7.97 lakh crore, surpassing corporate tax collections.

Tax Categories Breakdown

  • Corporate Tax: Increased by 8.6%, reaching ₹7.43 lakh crore.
  • Non-Corporate Tax: Grew by 22.5%, totaling ₹7.97 lakh crore.
  • Gross Tax Receipts: Increased by 20.3%, totaling ₹19.21 lakh crore.
  • Securities Transaction Tax (STT): Surged by 85.5%, exceeding ₹40,100 crore.
  • Refunds Issued: Grew by 42.5%, reaching ₹3.39 lakh crore.
  • Advance Tax Collections: Increased by 20.9%, totaling ₹7.56 lakh crore.

Historical Comparison

In the fiscal year 2023-24, net direct tax collections had risen by 17.7% to ₹19.58 lakh crore. Other taxes such as Equalization Levy, Fringe Benefit Tax, and Wealth Tax saw a modest growth of 5.9%.
