DAE-Homi Bhabha Chair Scheme

The Department of Atomic Energy (DAE) honors distinguished scientists and engineers through the DAE-Homi Bhabha Chair Scheme, recognizing those who have made significant contributions to critical technologies.

Key Features

  • Objective: Support outstanding scientists/engineers for continued research.
  • Eligibility: Retired professionals involved in sensitive technologies.
  • Honorarium: ₹2,00,000/- per month (adjusted with pension if applicable).
  • Contingency Grant: ₹76,000/- for secretarial assistance, etc.
  • Tenure: 1-5 years based on the selection committee’s discretion.

Department of Atomic Energy (DAE)

Founded in 1954 under Jawaharlal Nehru, DAE focuses on nuclear power, radiation technology, and national security.

About Homi Bhabha

Homi Jehangir Bhabha (1909-1966), known as the "Father of the Indian Nuclear Program," conceptualized India’s atomic energy program and established critical institutions like the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research.

Legacy

The Bhabha Atomic Research Centre and the DAE-Homi Bhabha Chair Scheme are named in his honor.
