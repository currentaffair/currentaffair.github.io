CCTNS: Connecting Police Stations

Key Features of CCTNS

  • Maintains crime records like accused individuals, habitual offenders, and stolen vehicles.
  • Generates analytical reports for crime and property statistics.
  • Integrates police data with courts, prisons, forensics, and more through ICJS.
  • Updates to include new provisions for arrests, videography, and forensic evidence.

Open Prisons in India

Open prisons offer a rehabilitation-focused alternative, allowing prisoners to engage in activities like farming. Currently, India has 91 open prisons, with Rajasthan housing the most.

Challenges & Improvements for Open Prisons

  • Challenges: Underutilization, inconsistent eligibility criteria, and lack of uniform rules across states.
  • Improvements: Relax eligibility, expand facilities, and include prison reform on the Union list.

Way Forward

Open prisons play a vital role in prisoner rehabilitation and reducing overcrowding. Expanding and improving the system can help lower custodial death rates and improve social reintegration.
