Hubble Tension and Universe Expansion

Recent observations from NASA's James Webb Space Telescope support the Hubble Tension, a discrepancy in the universe's expansion rate. The universe is expanding about 8% faster than previously predicted, a finding consistent with the Hubble Space Telescope's data. This raises questions about dark energy and dark matter, which make up 96% of the cosmos but remain largely mysterious.

Energy Conservation Day 2024

The National Energy Conservation Day on December 14th saw the Vice President of India honoring winners of the National Painting Competition. The event highlighted energy conservation initiatives under the Energy Conservation (Amendment) Act of 2022. The Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) also awarded 71 achievements for reducing energy consumption across sectors, as part of the National Energy Conservation Awards 2024.
