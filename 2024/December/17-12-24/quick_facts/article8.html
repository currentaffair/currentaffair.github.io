Indian Light Tank (ILT) Zorawar

The Indian Light Tank (ILT), also known as Zorawar, successfully completed firing trials at an altitude of over 4,200 meters.

Key Features

  • High-Altitude Performance: Demonstrated exceptional mobility and reliability in Ladakh's challenging terrain, firing multiple rounds with precision.
  • Airlift Capability: Successfully airlifted by the Indian Air Force for rapid deployment to remote areas.

Significance

The ILT strengthens India's mountain warfare capabilities through indigenous innovation, offering air transportability, high-angle firing, and limited artillery roles for enhanced mobility.
