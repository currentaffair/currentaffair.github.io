Malibu Wildfire - Franklin Fire

Overview

The Franklin Fire recently erupted in Malibu, California, prompting evacuations. Experts attribute its destructiveness to Santa Ana winds and climate change.

Santa Ana Winds

These winds occur when high pressure over the Great Basin and low pressure over California drive strong winds from inland deserts to the Pacific Ocean. They typically occur from October to January.

Wildfire Definition

A wildfire is an uncontrolled fire in natural areas like forests or grasslands, spreading due to environmental factors such as wind and terrain.

Indian Scenario

According to the ISFR 2021 by the Forest Survey of India (FSI), 35.47% of forest cover in India is fire-prone. The highest number of forest fires are in Mizoram, Manipur, Assam, Meghalaya, and Maharashtra.

Government Initiatives

The Indian government has initiated the National Action Plan for Forest Fires (NAPFF) and the Forest Fire Prevention and Management Scheme (FPM) to tackle this issue.
